{{nihongo|'''Masahiro Yamada'''|山田 昌弘|Yamada Masahiro|extra=born November 30, 1957}} is a [[Japan]]ese [[sociologist]]<ref name="Profile">http://c-faculty.chuo-u.ac.jp/~m-yamada/kenkyu/pro06.html</ref> known for coining popular sociological terms like ''[[parasite singles]]'' and {{nihongo|''[[Social inequality|gap-widening society]]''<ref>translation reference: http://orangekick.blog19.fc2.com/blog-entry-149.html;<br/>{{cite web |url=http://www.eigotown.com/eigocollege/neologism/backnumber/neologism_15.shtml |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2007-09-08 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080408124014/http://www.eigotown.com/eigocollege/neologism/backnumber/neologism_15.shtml |archivedate=2008-04-08 }}</ref>|格差社会|kakusa shakai}}. A graduate of [[University of Tokyo]],<ref name="Profile"/> he now teaches at [[Tokyo Gakugei University]] as professor.
