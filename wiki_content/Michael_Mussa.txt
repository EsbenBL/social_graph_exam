'''Michael Louis Mussa''' (April 15, 1944 – January 15, 2012) was an American [[economist]] and academic. He was [[List of IMF Economic Counsellors|chief economist]] at the [[International Monetary Fund]] from 1991 to 2001 and was a member of the [[Council of Economic Advisers]] from 1986 to 1988. He was also a senior fellow at the [[Peterson Institute for International Economics]] from 2001 until his death in 2012.<ref>[https://www.nytimes.com/2012/01/19/business/michael-mussa-adviser-to-reagan-on-economy-dies-at-67.html Michael Mussa, Adviser to Reagan on Economy, Dies at 67] ''NY Times,'' January 18, 2012</ref><ref>[https://blogs.wsj.com/economics/2012/01/17/remembering-economist-mike-mussa/ Remembering Economist Mike Mussa] ''The Wall Street Journal,'' January 17, 2012</ref><ref>[https://www.washingtonpost.com/local/obituaries/michael-l-mussa-imf-economist-dies-at-67/2012/01/17/gIQAQM9m6P_story.html Michael L. Mussa, IMF economist, dies at 67] ''Washington Post,'' January 18, 2012</ref>
