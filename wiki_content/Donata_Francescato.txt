'''Donata Francescato''' (born April 17, 1944 in [[Arona, Piedmont|Arona]], [[Italy]]) is an Italian [[Community psychology|community psychologist]] and academic, trained in the United States, and known in academia and the media for her work on sex roles, communes, and personal and organizational empowerment. Her work in community psychology led to the discipline being recognized in 1985 as a compulsory subject in Italian universities where psychology is taught as a major subject.<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ZJkxokvr75wC&q=community+psychology+1985+italy|title=Community Psychology: Challenges, Controversies and Emerging Consensus|last=Orford|first=Jim|date=2008-04-15|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=9780470855959|pages=xviii|language=en}}</ref>

Francescato is currently the Scientific Director of the post-graduate School of Specialization in clinical community psychology and psychotherapy at ASPIC (Associazione per lo Sviluppo Psicologico dell'Individuo e della Comunità, ''Association for the Psychological Development of the Individual and the Community'').<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.aspicpsicologia.org/formazione/istituto-per-la-formazione-di-psicoterapeuti.html|title=Istituto per la formazione di psicoterapeuti - Associazione ASPIC PSICOLOGIA|website=www.aspicpsicologia.org|language=it|access-date=2017-04-03}}</ref> Previously, she was a Full Professor of Community Psychology at the [[Sapienza University of Rome|University of Rome]], a position she retired from in 2014 at the age of 70.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.psicologia1.uniroma1.it/static/didattica/IdDocente_63.shtml|title=Didattica - Facoltà di Medicina e Psicologia|website=www.psicologia1.uniroma1.it|access-date=2017-04-03}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=Affective Education in Europe|last=Lang|first=Peter|publisher=Bloomsbury Publishing|year=1998|pages=ix}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Francescato|first=Donata|title=Strengthening community psychology in Europe through increasing professional competencies for the new Territorial Community Psychologists|url=http://www.gjcpp.org/en/article.php?issue=25&article=160|journal=Global Journal of Community Psychology Practice|quote=She [Francescato] was the first Italian Full Professor of Community Psychology at Rome Sapienza University retiring in 2014.}}</ref>

== Background ==
Francescato earned her B.A. in French literature from [[Rice University]] in 1967.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://space.rice.edu/alumni/pdf/rice_commencement_1967.pdf|title=Rice University Fifty-fourth Commencement Exercises Program}}</ref> She later went on to receive an M.A. in French literature from Rice University in 1970,<ref>{{Cite thesis|title=Alain Robbe-Grillet and Nathalie Sarraute: two aspects of the new novel|year=1969|type=Thesis|hdl = 1911/90046}}</ref> and a PhD in clinical psychology from the [[University of Houston]] in 1972.<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite book|title=International Community Psychology: History and Theories|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|year=2007|editor-last=Reich|editor-first=Stephanie|pages=364}}</ref> Her post-doctoral fellowship was undertaken at [[Brandeis University]] as a research associate in the Sociology department.<ref name=":0" />

Upon her return to Italy, she wrote the first Italian textbook on community psychology and co-founded ''Effe'', a feminist magazine active from 1972 to 1982.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://efferivistafemminista.it/writer/donata-francescato/|title=Donata Francescato|work=Effe Rivista Femminista|access-date=2017-04-18|language=it-IT}}</ref> She also co-founded the European Network of Community Psychologists (ENCP) which gave birth to the European Association Community Association (ECPA).<ref name=":1">{{Cite book|title=Psicologia Comunitária|last=Ornelas|first=José|publisher=Fim de Século Edições|year=2008|isbn=9789727542567|location=Lisbon, Portugal|pages=382}}</ref> She is a member of the editorial boards of the ''Journal of Community and Applied Social Psychology'', the ''Global Journal of Community Psychology Practice,'' ''Psicologia di Comunità (Community Psychology)'' and ''Psicoterapia Familiare (Family Psychotherapy).''<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Journal of Community & Applied Social Psychology - Editorial Board - Wiley Online Library|url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/journal/10.1002/(ISSN)1099-1298/homepage/EditorialBoard.html|language=en|doi=10.1002/(ISSN)1099-1298}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.gjcpp.org/en/management-structure.php|title=Global Journal of Community Psychology Practice|website=www.gjcpp.org|access-date=2017-04-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://francoangeli.it/riviste/sommario.asp?anno=2005&idRivista=139&lingua=en|title=PSICOLOGIA DI COMUNITA'|website=francoangeli.it|language=it|access-date=2017-04-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://francoangeli.it/riviste/sommario.asp?idRivista=68&lingua=en|title=TERAPIA FAMILIARE|website=francoangeli.it|language=it|access-date=2017-04-18}}</ref>

During her academic career Francescato developed the concept and practice of innovative online teaching methodologies to teach professional competences using [[computer-supported collaborative learning]]. She also developed intervention methodologies to promote individual, group, organizational and community empowerment, as well as many intervention programs for the empowerment of women, immigrants and members of disadvantaged communities. One prong of her research involves evaluating these methodologies.<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite journal|last1=Francescato|first1=Donata|last2=Tomai|first2=Manuela|last3=Burattini|first3=Monica|last4=Rosa|first4=Veronica|date=2000|title=Affective educations as a strategy for primary and secondary preventions in underprivileged schools and communities of Italy|journal=Curriculum and Teaching|volume=15|issue=13|pages=103–115|doi=10.7459/ct/15.2.09}}</ref>

== Contributions ==
Francescato's contributions fall in three main areas. In [[women's studies]], she investigates sex roles in families and communes; gender differences among activists, local and national politicians; dual career families; single childrearing; and separation and divorce processes.<ref>{{Cite book|title=Psicologia della politica|last1=Francescato|first1=Donata|last2=Mebane|first2=Minou|publisher=Raffaello Cortina|year=2011|isbn=9788860304001|editor-last=Catellani|editor-first=Patrizia|location=Milano|pages=253–270|chapter=Donne Politische|editor-last2=Sensales|editor-first2=Gilda}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=Famiglie, reti familiari e cohousing|last=Francescato|first=Donata|publisher=Angeli|year=2010|isbn=9788856825183|editor-last=Sapio|editor-first=Antonella|location=Milano|pages=170–185|chapter=Dalle comuni degli anni 60 al cohousing del 2000}}</ref> She is currently studying gender differences in aging processes, studying triads of caregiver/elder/most involved relative, as well as triads of grandparent/parent/adult grandchild, for effects on systemic wellbeing and intergenerational solidarity.<ref>{{Cite journal|last1=Francescato|first1=Donata|last2=Pezzuti|first2=Lina|last3=Mebane|first3=Minou|last4=Tomai|first4=Manuela|last5=Benedetti|first5=Maura|last6=Moro|first6=Annalisa|date=2016-06-01|title=Dispositional characteristics, relational well-being and perceived life satisfaction and empowerment of elders|journal=Aging & Mental Health|volume=21|issue=10|pages=1052–1057|doi=10.1080/13607863.2016.1191058|issn=1360-7863|pmid=27248667|s2cid=4967290}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last1=Francescato|first1=Donata|last2=Mebane|first2=Minou|last3=Pezzuti|first3=Lina|title=Nonni e nipoti adulti: verso nuovi pattern di solidarietà intergenerazionale|journal=Terapia Familiare|issue=112|pages=7–30|doi=10.3280/tf2016-112002|year=2016}}</ref>
