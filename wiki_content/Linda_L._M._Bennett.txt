'''Linda Louise M. Bennett''' (born December 25, 1952 in [[Cincinnati]]) was named president of the [[University of Southern Indiana]] (USI) by its Board of [[Trustees]] effective 2009 July 1, incumbent president H. Ray Hoops retiring on 2009 June 30.<ref>[http://www.courierpress.com/news/2009/jan/31/bennett-named-new-usi-president/ Bennett named new USI president] in ''Courier & Press'' (Evansville, Indiana), 2009 January 31; [http://www.insideindianabusiness.com/newsitem.asp?ID=33745 "USI names new president"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110928200053/http://www.insideindianabusiness.com/newsitem.asp?ID=33745 |date=2011-09-28 }} in ''Inside Indiana Business'', 2009 February 2. At the time of her selection to be president of USI, Bennett was one of five remaining applicants under consideration for the presidency of [[Southeastern Louisiana University]] at [[Hammond, Louisiana|Hammond]]; see [http://www.nbc33tv.com/news/five-finalists-named-slu-presidency "Five finalists named for Southeastern Louisiana University presidency"] as reported by [[WVLA]] Television Channel 33 ([[NBC-TV]] affiliate in [[Baton Rouge]]).</ref>

Bennett previously served at [[Wittenberg University]], [[Northern Kentucky University]], and [[Appalachian State University]] and as USI's [[Provost (education)|provost]] and vice president for academic affairs. A professor of [[political science]], Bennett has a publication record which includes joint-authorship, with her husband Stephen Earl Bennett, of ''Living with Leviathan: Americans Coming to Terms with Big Government'' (2007).<ref>Lawrence: University Press of Kansas.  {{ISBN|978-0-7006-0432-6}} (hardbound), {{ISBN|978-0-7006-0433-3}} (paperbound).</ref> According to [https://web.archive.org/web/20100611091234/http://www.polisci.uc.edu/CV/MargolisVita.0106.pdf Michael Margolis], 
::No other book has ordered and analyzed a comparable set of data regarding attitudes toward the power of the federal government.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.kansaspress.ku.edu/benliv.html |title=University Press of Kansas book site. |access-date=2009-02-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100626200932/http://www.kansaspress.ku.edu/benliv.html |archive-date=2010-06-26 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
 
Linda Bennett received her baccalaureate and master's degrees and her Ph.D. in political science all from the [[University of Cincinnati]].  The Bennetts reside in [[Evansville, Indiana]].<ref>[http://www.usi.edu/acaffrs/BennettVita.htm Linda Bennett's ''curriculum vitae''.] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080517044140/http://www.usi.edu/acaffrs/BennettVita.htm |date=2008-05-17 }}</ref>

On August 15, 2017, Bennett announced she would retire as president USI on June 30, 2018.<ref>[http://www.courierpress.com/story/news/education/2017/08/15/usi-president-retiring-2018/104616694/] USI president Linda Bennett to retire</ref>
