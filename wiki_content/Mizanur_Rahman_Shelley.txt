'''Mizanur Rahman Shelley''' (2 January 1943 – 12 August 2019)<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.newagebd.net/article/896/articlelist/323/Cartoon |title=The symphony of our times |work=New Age |date=16 October 2016 |access-date=15 August 2019}}</ref> was a minister of [[Government of Bangladesh]], political analyst, political scientist and educationalist.<ref name=a/>

==Biography==
Shelley was born on 2 January 1943 in [[Munshiganj District]]'s Kusumpur Village.<ref name=b>{{cite news |url=https://m.bdnews24.com/bn/detail/bangladesh/1653674 |script-title=bn:সাবেক মন্ত্রী মিজানূর রহমান শেলীর মৃত্যু |language=bn |work=bdnews24.com |date=12 August 2019 |access-date=12 August 2019}}</ref> After completing study from [[University of Dhaka]] he joined its Political Science Department as a teacher.<ref name=c>{{cite news |script-title=bn:চলে গেলেন সাবেক তথ্যমন্ত্রী মিজানুর রহমান শেলী |language=bn |url=https://www.ittefaq.com.bd/capital/79490/চলে-গেলেন-সাবেক-তথ্যমন্ত্রী-মিজানুর-রহমান-শেলী |work=The Daily Ittefaq |date=12 August 2019 |access-date=12 August 2019}}</ref><ref name=d>{{cite news |url=https://www.jagonews24.com/m/national/news/520001 |script-title=bn:সাবেক তথ্যমন্ত্রী মিজানুর রহমান শেলী আর নেই |language=bn |work=Jago News 24 |date=12 August 2019 |access-date=12 August 2019}}</ref> In 1967, he resigned from the post and joined Government Service.<ref name=b/> After joining Government Service he obtained a PhD from the [[University of London]] in International Politics. He resigned from Government Service in 1980. He was the Director of Social Welfare Department at that time. He served as Minister of [[Ministry of Information (Bangladesh)|Information Ministry]] and [[Ministry of Water Resources (Bangladesh)|Water Resources Ministry]] in 1990.<ref name=b/><ref name=c/><ref name=e>{{cite news |url=https://www.jugantor.com/national/209514/সাবেক-তথ্যমন্ত্রী-মিজানূর-রহমান-শেলী-আর-নেই |language=bn |script-title=bn:সাবেক তথ্যমন্ত্রী মিজানূর রহমান শেলী আর নেই |work=Jugantor |date=12 August 2019 |access-date=12 August 2019}}</ref>

Shelley was the Founding Chairman of the Center for Development Research Bangladesh and editor of Asian Affairs.<ref name=b/><ref name=c/> He was also the Chairman of Premier Leasing and Finance.<ref name=e/> He was the Advisory Editor of Weekly ''Sochitro Swadesh'' and Trust Chairman of the ''Bangladesh Times''.<ref name=b/> His writings were published in national dailies of Bangladesh. He wrote books about sociology and politics. He also wrote books covering other topics. In 2008 he received an honorary fellowship from the [[Bangla Academy]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://banglaacademy.portal.gov.bd/site/page/02bcd06a-3019-4102-963b-bdcc2530531d |script-title=bn:বাংলা একাডেমি প্রদত্ত অন্যান্য পুরস্কার ও সম্মানসূচক ফেলোশিপপ্রাপ্ত বিশিষ্টজনের তালিকা |website=Bangla Academy |language=bn |access-date=14 August 2019}}</ref>

Shelley was married to Sufia Rahman. She died in 2016.<ref name=b/><ref name=e/> They had two sons. Their names are Tahmid Ibne Mizan and Arif Ibne Mizan.<ref name=e/>

Shelley died in Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University Hospital of Dhaka on 12 August 2019 at the age of 76.<ref name=a>{{cite news |url=https://m.banglanews24.com/cat/news/bd/733567.details |script-title=bn:মিজানুর রহমান শেলী মারা গেছেন |language=bn |work=Banglanews24.com |date=12 August 2019 |access-date=12 August 2019}}</ref><ref name=f>{{cite news |url=https://www.kalerkantho.com/online/national/2019/08/12/802834 |language=bn |script-title=bn:সাবেক তথ্যমন্ত্রী মিজানুর রহমান শেলী আর নেই |work=Kaler Kantho |date=12 August 2019 |access-date=12 August 2019}}</ref>
