'''Averil Leimon''' is an author,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www2.lse.ac.uk/newsAndMedia/news/archives/2010/12/coachingWomen.aspx |title=Support from senior women is essential to increase the number of women leaders – 12 – 2010 – News archive – News – News and media – Home |publisher=.lse.ac.uk |date=13 February 2012 |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref> executive [[Coaching|coach]],<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.associationforcoaching.com/dir/acc.htm#1_4 |title=Association for Coaching |publisher=Association for Coaching |access-date=16 June 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120219015316/http://www.associationforcoaching.com/dir/acc.htm#1_4 |archive-date=19 February 2012 |url-status=dead }}</ref> leadership [[psychologist]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/1247409.stm |title=UK &#124; Stop all the clocks |publisher=BBC News |date=28 March 2001 |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref> and was one of the first UK-based psychologists qualified in the academic field of [[positive psychology]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://acukconference.com/2012/keynote-sessions/positive-psychology/ |archive-url=https://archive.is/20130116051414/http://acukconference.com/2012/keynote-sessions/positive-psychology/ |url-status=dead |archive-date=16 January 2013 |title=Positive Psychology – Hope for the Future? – Association For Coaching UK Conference |publisher=Acukconference.com |access-date=16 June 2012 }}</ref> Leimon is the joint editor of ''The Essential Coaching Series'' from academic publisher [[Routledge]] and is co-author of ''Essential Business Coaching''<ref>{{Cite book|isbn=978-1583918838|title=Essential Business Coaching (Essential Coaching Skills and Knowledge) (9781583918838): Averi Leimon, Francois Moscovici, Gladeana Mcmahon: Books |publisher=Amazon.com }}</ref> and ''Coaching Women to Lead''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.amazon.com/Coaching-Essential-Skills-Knowledge-ebook/dp/B0045U9VOC/ref=sr_1_5?ie=UTF8&qid=1338288372&sr=8-5 |title=Coaching Women to Lead (Essential Coaching Skills and Knowledge) eBook: Averil Leimon, Helen Goodier, François Moscovici: Kindle Store |publisher=Amazon.com |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref> She is also the co-author of ''Positive Psychology for Dummies''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.amazon.com/Positive-Psychology-For-Dummies-ebook/dp/B004KKY1EM/ref=sr_1_6?ie=UTF8&qid=1338288372&sr=8-6 |title=Positive Psychology For Dummies eBook: Gladeana McMahon, Averil Leimon: Kindle Store |publisher=Amazon.com |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref> and ''Performance Coaching for Dummies''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://uk.nicebooks.com/search?q=Averil+Leimon |title=Averil Leimon – NiceBooks |publisher=Uk.nicebooks.com |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref> and the author of 100 lessons on happiness in 100 words or less.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.amazon.co.uk/Lessons-Happiness-Words-Less-ebook/dp/B005C65UVG/ref=sr_1_7?ie=UTF8&qid=1337184893&sr=8-7 |title=100 Lessons on Happiness in 100 Words or Less (100 Lessons in 100 Words or Less) eBook: Averil Leimon: Amazon.co.uk: Kindle Store |publisher=Amazon.co.uk |date=10 July 2011 |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref>

Leimon is also co-founder of business consultancy White Water Group.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bps.org.uk/news/older-people-have-better-life-satisfaction |title=Older people have better life satisfaction |publisher=BPS |date=29 February 2012 |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref>

== Biography ==
Leimon gained a M.Phil in Clinical Psychology at the University of Glasgow and a BA (Honours) in Psychology at Strathclyde University. She is a major contributor of psychological insight to television (BBC, CNN),<ref>{{cite web|last=Holt |first=Gerry |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-18127469 |title=BBC News – Are women their own worst enemy when it comes to the top jobs? |publisher=Bbc.co.uk |date=29 May 2012 |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://edition.cnn.com/video/#/video/living/2008/03/07/inv.what.is.happiness.leimon.cnn?iref=allsearch |title=What is Happiness? |publisher=Cable News Network |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120529105010/http://edition.cnn.com/video/ |archive-date=29 May 2012 }}</ref> radio (BBC Radio 4 ''[[Woman's Hour]]''),<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b016lf2n/ |title=Radio 4 Programmes – Woman's Hour, 02/11/2011 |publisher=BBC |date=2 November 2011 |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref> newspapers (FT, City A.M)<ref>{{cite web|author=Juliet Samuel |url=http://www.cityam.com/the-capitalist/women-boards-healthy-boys-girls-and-the-bottom-line |title=Women on Boards: Healthy For Boys, Girls And The Bottom Line &#124; City A.M |publisher=City A.M. |date=6 December 2010 |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref> and magazines (Women in Business, Cosmopolitan).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cosmopolitan.co.za/bodysoul/MindHealth/id-your-secret-stress-factors-pg2 |title=Mind Health &#124; Body & Soul &#124; ID Your Secret Stress Factors |publisher=Cosmopolitan |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref>&nbsp; She is also a member of and a speaker for the [[British Psychological Society]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/health/4577392.stm |title=Health &#124; Why hard work makes people happy |publisher=BBC News |date=3 January 2006 |access-date=16 June 2012}}</ref>
