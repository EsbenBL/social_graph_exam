'''Gopal Krishna Sarangi''' is an Indian economist specializing in [[energy economics]], [[climate change]],<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://www.indiaeu-climategovernance.org/people/researchers/sarangi/index.html | title=Gopalkrishna Sarangi| date=2013-01-05}}</ref> energy regulations, energy access, etc.<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://www.icrn.in/user/4701 | title=Gopal.sarangi &#124; Indian Climate Research Network}}</ref> He is at present an Assistant Professor at Department of Policy Studies, [[Teri University]].<ref>http://www.teriuniversity.ac.in/index.php?option=com_faculty&task=aboutcv&fid=S107</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url=https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Gopal_K_Sarangi/publications | title=Gopal K. Sarangi &#124; PhD &#124; TERI University, New Delhi &#124; Department of Policy Studies}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url=http://works.bepress.com/gopal_sarangi/ | title=SelectedWorks - Gopal Krishna Sarangi}}</ref>

==Education==
His doctoral thesis at Teri University was entitled, "Electricity sector regulation and sustainable development outcomes: An analysis of regulatory impact in 12 Indian states for 2001–2010." under the supervision of Professor Arabinda Mishra.<ref>http://www.teriuniversity.ac.in/templates/teriuniv14/pdf/TU-Annual-Report-2015.pdf</ref> He had earlier pursued his Master's in Economics from [[Ravenshaw University]], [[Odisha]].
