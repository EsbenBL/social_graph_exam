'''Dianne Marie Pinderhughes''' (born 1947)<ref name="VIAF">{{cite web|title=Pinderhughes, Dianne M. (Dianne Marie), 1947-|url=http://viaf.org/viaf/244458221/#Pinderhughes,_Dianne_M._%28Dianne_Marie%29,_1947-|publisher=Virtual International Authority File (VIAF)|access-date=3 June 2014}}</ref> is Full Professor in the Departments of [[Africana Studies]] and [[Political Science]] at the [[University of Notre Dame]], and former President of the [[American Political Science Association]]. Since 2021 she is the president of the [[International Political Science Association]]. She holds a [[Bachelor of Arts|B.A.]] from [[Albertus Magnus College]] and an [[Master of Arts|M.A.]] and [[Ph.D.]] in Political Science from the [[University of Chicago]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.apsanet.org/content_38005.cfm|title=Dianne Pinderhughes, President APSA|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070204003459/http://www.apsanet.org/content_38005.cfm|archive-date=2007-02-04|website=American Political Science Association (APSA)}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://africana.nd.edu/about/faculty-staff/Pinderhughes.shtml |title=Dianne Pinderhughes at nd.edu |access-date=2008-10-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080820014421/http://africana.nd.edu/about/faculty-staff/Pinderhughes.shtml|archive-date=2008-08-20 |url-status=dead }}</ref> Pinderhughes sits on the editorial board of the ''[[Journal of Women, Politics & Policy]]''.<ref name="Ed_board">{{cite web|title=Journal of Women, Politics & Policy - Editorial board |url=http://www.tandfonline.com/action/journalInformation?show=editorialBoard&journalCode=wwap20|publisher=Taylor and Francis |access-date=3 June 2014}}</ref> She was American Academy of Arts and Sciences Fellow of 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://members.amacad.org/content/members/newFellows.aspx|title=2019 Fellows and International Honorary Members with their affiliations at the time of election|website=members.amacad.org|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200302174101/https://members.amacad.org/content/members/newFellows.aspx|archive-date=2020-03-02}}</ref>
