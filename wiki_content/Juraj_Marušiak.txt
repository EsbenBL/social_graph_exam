'''Juraj Marusiak''' PhD. (in [[Slovak language|Slovak]]: ''Juraj Marušiak''; born 10 July 1970, in [[Bratislava]]) is a [[Slovak people|Slovak]] [[political scientist]], [[historian]], [[journalist]] and translator of a mixed Slovak-Polish descent. He represents a moderate social-democratic stream in Slovak political thinking.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.blisty.cz/aut/590/art.html|title=Juraj Marušiak - autor - Britské listy|website=Britské listy}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.muni.cz/vyzkum/publikace/634396|title=Smer - From Pragamtism to Social Democracy? Seeking Identity|first1=Juraj|last1=Marušiak|first2=Lubomír|last2=Kopeček|date=21 July 2018|publisher=CDK a ISPO|via=www.muni.cz}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.cec-wys.org/html/index.php?s1=1&s2=4&s3=19&s4=0&s5=0&s6=0&lng=13&user_url=&menu_id=1&m=1&typ=clanky&recid_cl=1927&menu_id=1&PHPSESSID=a528121c7610efac4fe3b22918b81bc6 |title=Archived copy |access-date=2008-07-22 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110725151440/http://www.cec-wys.org/html/index.php?s1=1&s2=4&s3=19&s4=0&s5=0&s6=0&lng=13&user_url=&menu_id=1&m=1&typ=clanky&recid_cl=1927&menu_id=1&PHPSESSID=a528121c7610efac4fe3b22918b81bc6 |archive-date=2011-07-25 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

== Life ==
Marušiak graduated from the [[Faculty of Arts, Comenius University|Faculty of Arts of Comenius University in Bratislava]] (1994). Since 1996 he has worked for the [[Institute of Political Science of SAS]] (the [[Slovak Academy of Science]]), where he obtained a PhD in 2003.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.upv.sav.sk/index.php?ID=19|title=Mgr. Juraj MARUŠIAK, PhD.|first=AGLO solutions|last=(www.aglo.sk)|website=www.upv.sav.sk}}</ref> From 2000 until 2009 he was a Scientific Secretary of this Institute and since 2009 he has been head of the Scientific Board. From 2004 to 2010 he was chairman of the Society for Central and Eastern Europe.{{citation needed|date=April 2010}}. In 2013 - 2021 he was a member of the Presidium of Slovak Academy of Sciences.

== Publications ==

''Slovenská literatúra a moc v druhej polovici 50.rokov'' (Slovak Literature and Political Power in 1950s), Brno, 2001, 181 pp.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bookfinder.com/author/juraj-marusiak/|title=BookFinder.com: New & Used Books, Rare Books, Textbooks, Out of Print Books|website=www.bookfinder.com}}</ref>

His professional articles are mostly about problems of Central and Eastern Europe in 20th and 21st centuries.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sav.sk/index.php?lang=sk&doc=user-org-user&user_no=9231&action=publications|title=SAV - Organizácie SAV - Zamestnanci SAV: Juraj Marušiak|first=vs|last=sav|website=SAV - Organizácie SAV - Zamestnanci SAV: Juraj Marušiak}}</ref>
