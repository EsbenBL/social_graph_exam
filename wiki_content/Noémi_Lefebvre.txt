{{short description|French writer (born 1964)}}
'''Noémi Lefebvre''' (born 1964) is a [[France|French]] writer. She studied music and politics, eventually becoming a political scientist at the [[Instituts d'études politiques]] of [[Grenoble II]]. She has written several books, among them ''L’autoportrait bleu'' (2009), her debut novel which has been translated into English by [[Sophie Lewis]]; ''L'état des sentiments à l'âge adulte'' (2012); ''L’enfance politique'' (2015) and ''Poétique de l'emploi'' (2018). 

She was born in [[Caen]] and lives in [[Lyon]].<ref>[http://www.editions-verticales.com/auteurs_fiche.php?id=153&rubrique=4 Profile]</ref>
