'''Marjorie Harness Goodwin''' is an American anthropologist, currently Distinguished Professor at [[University of California, Los Angeles]],<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.anthro.ucla.edu/faculty/marjorie-harness-goodwin |title=Marjorie Harness Goodwin |publisher=ucla.edu |accessdate=August 5, 2016}}</ref> and also a published author of books.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.worldcat.org/identities/lccn-n87107801/ |title=Goodwin, Marjorie Harness |publisher=worldcat.org |accessdate=August 5, 2016}}</ref>

==Education==
She received her PhD from the [[University of Pennsylvania]] and her honorary PhD from [[Uppsala University]].
