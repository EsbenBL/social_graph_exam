'''Clyde Prestowitz''' (born 1941) is the founder and President of the [[Economic Strategy Institute]]. He formerly served as counselor to the [[Secretary of Commerce]] in the [[Reagan Administration]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.econstrat.org/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=20&Itemid=50|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081127023838/http://www.econstrat.org/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=20&Itemid=50|url-status=dead|archive-date=2008-11-27|title=About Us|website=www.econstrat.org}}</ref>  He is a labor economist.<ref>{{cite news| url=http://business.time.com/2012/01/27/are-companies-more-powerful-than-countries/ |title=Bio| work=[[Time (magazine)|Time]] | date=27 January 2012}}</ref>  Prestowitz has written for [[Foreign Affairs]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.foreignaffairs.com/search/Clyde%2BV.%2BPrestowitz%2C%2BJr.|title=Search|website=Foreign Affairs}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Prestowitz was born to a family with a conservative Republican and evangelical Christian background<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.clydeprestowitz.com/cprestowitz-rogue-backstory.htm |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2013-10-30 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131106015208/http://www.clydeprestowitz.com/cprestowitz-rogue-backstory.htm |archivedate=2013-11-06 }}</ref><ref name=utah/>  and earned a B.A. with honors from [[Swarthmore College]]; an M.A. in East-West Policies and Economics from the East-West Center of the [[University of Hawaii]]; and an M.B.A. from the [[Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania|Wharton School]] of the [[University of Pennsylvania]].<ref name=utah>{{Cite web |url=http://archives.econ.utah.edu/archives/pen-l/2003w27/msg00074.html |title=Archived copy |access-date=2013-10-30 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20131030095801/http://archives.econ.utah.edu/archives/pen-l/2003w27/msg00074.html |archive-date=2013-10-30 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref name="Prestowitz bio">{{cite web|url=http://mitworld.mit.edu/speaker/view/580|title=MIT World Speakers: Clyde V. Prestowitz Jr.|publisher=[[Massachusetts Institute of Technology]]|accessdate=2011-12-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110913040417/http://mitworld.mit.edu/speaker/view/580|archive-date=2011-09-13|url-status=dead}}</ref>
