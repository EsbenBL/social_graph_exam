'''Anthony Saunders''' is the John M. Schiff Professor of Finance at [[New York University Stern School of Business]] and is currently on the Executive Committee of the Salomon Center of the Study of Financial Institutions.  He teaches "Market and Liquidity Risk" in the Risk Management Open Enrollment program for [http://www.stern.nyu.edu/Executive/OpenEnrollment/index.htm Stern Executive Education].  Saunders also teaches for both the [[Master of Science in Global Finance (MSGF)]] and [[Master of Science in Risk Management Program for Executives]] (MSRM).  MSGF<ref>[http://globalfinance.bm.ust.hk/index.asp Master of Science in Global Finance] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090405040117/http://globalfinance.bm.ust.hk/index.asp |date=2009-04-05 }}</ref> is jointly offered by NYU Stern and the [[Hong Kong University of Science and Technology]]. MSRM <ref>[http://w4.stern.nyu.edu/academic/global/riskmanagement/ Master of Science in Risk Management for Executives Program]</ref> is offered by NYU Stern, in partnership with the [[Amsterdam Institute of Finance]].<ref>[http://www.aif.nl/ Amsterdam Institute of Finance]</ref>

Saunders has taught both undergraduate and graduate level courses at [[NYU]] since 1978, and his teaching and research have specialized in financial institutions and international banking.  He has also served as a visiting professor all over the world, including at [[INSEAD]], the [[Stockholm School of Economics]], and the [[University of Melbourne]].<ref name="w4.stern.nyu.edu">{{Cite web |url=http://w4.stern.nyu.edu/faculty/facultyindex.cgi?id=189 |title=Anthony Saunders’ profile at NYU Stern School of Business |access-date=2009-03-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100614172438/http://w4.stern.nyu.edu/faculty/facultyindex.cgi?id=189 |archive-date=2010-06-14 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

==Biography==
Saunders holds positions on the Board of Academic Consultants of the [[Federal Reserve Board of Governors]] as well as on the Council of Research Advisors for the [[Federal National Mortgage Association]].  In addition, he has acted as a visiting scholar at the comptroller of the Currency and at the Federal Monetary Fund.<ref name="w4.stern.nyu.edu" />

==Publications==
Saunders is an editor of the ''Journal of Banking and Finance'' and the ''Journal of Financial Market's'', ''Instruments and Institutions'', and is an associate editor of eight other journals, including ''Financial Management'' and the ''Journal of Money, Credit and Banking''.  He has written two books and over 70 books, and his research has been published in all of the major finance and banking journals and in several books.<ref name="w4.stern.nyu.edu" /><ref name="Anthony Saunders’ Biography">[http://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~asaunder/ Anthony Saunders’ Biography]</ref><ref>[https://www.amazon.com/s/ref=nb_ss_gw?url=search-alias%3Daps&field-keywords=anthony+saunders List of Books on Amazon]</ref>
