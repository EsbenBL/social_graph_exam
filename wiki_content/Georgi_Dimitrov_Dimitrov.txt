'''Georgi D. Dimitrov''' ({{lang-bg|Георги Д. Димитров}}) (born April 13, 1958) is a [[Bulgaria]]n [[sociologist]] and [[Professor]] at the [[European Studies]] department of [[Sofia University]]. Doctor Habilis in [[Sociology]] (2000). He works in the field of historical sociology. Major works on: history and sociology of sociology, education reform, [[modernity]], [[Culture of Europe|European civilisation]], Bulgarian society.
