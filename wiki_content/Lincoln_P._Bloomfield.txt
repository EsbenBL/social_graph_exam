==Biography==
Lincoln P. Bloomfield is the father of [[Lincoln P. Bloomfield Jr.]], a [[United States Defense Department]] and [[State Department]] official.

Bloomfield served five [[United States Secretary of State|United States Secretaries of State]], held an administrative State Department position in the early years of the [[United Nations]], and was director of global issues on the [[National Security Council]] during the [[Carter administration]].

Bloomfield was a member of the [[Council on Foreign Relations]].<ref>{{cite book |title=Annual Report, September 1, 1975-August 31, 1976 |date=1976 |publisher=[[Council on Foreign Relations]] |location=New York, NY |chapter=Membership Roster}}</ref>
