'''Knut Blind''' (born 1965) is a German [[economist]]. He is active in the fields of [[innovation economics]] focusing on regulation and [[standardization]].

==Career==
Knut Blind studied [[economics]], [[political science]] and [[psychology]] at the [[University of Freiburg]] in Germany and [[Brock University]] in Canada. He worked as a research assistant in the department of [[public finance]] at the University of Freiburg, where he was awarded his doctorate. Blind was promoted to professor at the [[University of Kassel]], Germany.<ref name="aw">{{Cite web|url=http://www.inno.tu-berlin.de/menue/ueber_uns/team/prof_knut_blind/parameter/en/|title=FG Innovationsökonomie: Prof. Dr. rer. pol.|website=www.inno.tu-berlin.de}}</ref> Since 2006, he has worked as a professor at the [[Technical University of Berlin]], Chair for Innovation Economics. Between 2008 and 2016 he has also been awarded the Endowed Chair in Standardisation at the [[Rotterdam School of Management]] by the [[Erasmus University Rotterdam]].<ref name="aw" /> Between 2010 and 2019 Knut Blind moved to the [[Fraunhofer Institute for Open Communication Systems|Fraunhofer Institute for Open Communications Systems FOKUS]] in Berlin. Since October 2019 Knut Blind is Head of the Business Unit Regulation and Innovation at the [[:de:Fraunhofer-Institut für System- und Innovationsforschung|Fraunhofer Institute for System and Innovations Research ISI]] in Karlsruhe.<ref name="aw" />

In 2012, he founded the [http://www.fnsev.de/sixcms/detail.php?id=230108&_areaid=149368&_rubid=230108 German Standardization Panel] with the German Society for the Promotion of Research on Standardization e.V. (FNS) and the [https://www.inno.tu-berlin.de/menue/berlin_innovation_panel/parameter/de/ Berlin Innovations Panel].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.zew.de/de/presse/pressearchiv/innovationsbericht-berlin-2013-berliner-wirtschaft-sehr-innovationsorientiert/|title=ZEW-Pressemitteilung: Innovationsbericht Berlin 2013 – Berliner Wirtschaft sehr innovationsorientiert|website=zew.de}}</ref><ref>{{citation|periodical=WiWo Gründer|title=Innovationsstudie: Warum Start-ups spezielle Nachbarn brauchen – WiWo Gründer|date=2017-01-17|language=German|url=http://gruender.wiwo.de/innovationsstudie-warum-start-ups-spezielle-nachbarn-brauchen/|access-date=2017-01-18
}}</ref> Both panels can already rely on the [http://ftp.zew.de/pub/zew-docs/dp/dp16080.pdf results of seven annual surveys].

==Publications==
Blind has published his work in several high-ranking international journals, including Research Policy, Industrial and Corporate Change, IEEE Transactions on Engineering Management, Journal of Technology Transfer, Telecommunications Policy and Journal of Productivity Analysis.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.inno.tu-berlin.de/menue/publikationen/parameter/en/|title=FG Innovationsökonomie: Papers|website=www.inno.tu-berlin.de}}</ref> For his lifetime achievement, he has been credited as one of the leading German economists and has been listed in the [[Handelsblatt]]-Management-Ranking since 2009, which was adopted by Wirtschaftswoche in 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://tool.handelsblatt.com/tabelle/?id=32&so=1a&pc=25&po=125|title=Handelsblatt Ranking Betriebswirtschaftslehre 2009 – Handelsblatt Online|website=tool.handelsblatt.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://tool.handelsblatt.com/tabelle/index.php?id=111&so=1a&pc=100&po=0|title=Handelsblatt-Ranking Betriebswirtschaftslehre 2012 – Handelsblatt Online|website=tool.handelsblatt.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://tool.handelsblatt.com/tabelle/index.php?id=141&so=1a&pc=25&po=50|title=Handelsblatt BWL-Ranking 2014 – Lebenswerk – Handelsblatt Online|website=tool.handelsblatt.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.forschungsmonitoring.org/ranking/bwl/lifetime|title=Forschungsmonitoring|website=www.forschungsmonitoring.org|access-date=2019-01-28}}</ref> Furthermore, in 2013, he was listed as one of the most influential German economist by the F.A.Z. Economic Rating.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pressestelle.tu-berlin.de/medieninformationen/2013/oktober_2013/medieninformation_nr_2122013/ |title=TU Berlin auf Platz 3 im F.A.Z.-Ökonomenranking |lang=de |trans-title=TU Berlin in 3rd place in the FAZ economist ranking |date=October 14, 2013 |website=www.pressestelle.tu-berlin.de}}</ref> In January 2017, Makronom lists Blind on position 78 of the most influential German-speaking economists on Twitter.<ref>{{citation |periodical=Makronom |title=Twitter-Ranking – Makronom |language=German |url=http://makronom.de/schwerpunkte/oekonomenszene/twitter-ranking |access-date=2017-01-26}}</ref> Blind  was awarded with the F.A. v. [[Friedrich von Hayek|Hayek]]-Prize by the Department of Economics at the University of Freiburg for his dissertation.<ref name="aw" />
