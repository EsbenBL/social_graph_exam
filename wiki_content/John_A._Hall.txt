'''John A. Hall''' {{post-nominals|country=CAN|FRSC}} (born 1949) is the James McGill Professor of Comparative Historical Sociology at [[McGill University]], Montreal. He is the  author or editor of over 20 books.

==Education==
*PhD, [[London School of Economics]], 1976;
*MA, [[Pennsylvania State University]], 1972;
*BA, [[Oxford University]], 1970.

==Honours==
In 2012, Hall was named a Fellow of the [[Royal Society of Canada]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Search Fellows|url=http://rsc-src.ca/en/search-fellows|website=Royal Society of Canada|access-date=31 August 2017}}</ref> The RSC also presented him with the [[Innis-GÃ©rin Medal]] in 2016.<ref>{{cite web|title=Past Awards Winners|url=https://rsc-src.ca/en/past-award-winners#InnisGerin|website=Royal Society of Canada|access-date=31 August 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160602175348/http://rsc-src.ca/en/past-award-winners#InnisGerin|archive-date=2 June 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref>
