'''Ernst Angel''' (11 August 1894, [[Vienna, Austria]] â€“ 10 January 1986, [[Newark, New Jersey|Newark]], [[New Jersey]]) was an Austrian-born [[poet]], theatre and film [[critic]], screen play author, [[film director]] and publisher who later became a [[psychologist]]. He was [[Jewish]].<ref>[[Siegbert Salomon Prawer]], ''Between Two Worlds: The Jewish Presence in German and Austrian Film, 1910-1933'', [[Berghahn Books]] (2007), p. 211</ref>

For a period of time, he worked with [[Rollo May]], co-authoring a book.

He died in [[Newark Liberty International Airport|Newark Airport]].
