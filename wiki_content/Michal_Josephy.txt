'''Michal Josephy''', [[Ph.D]]. (born 1977) is an anthropologist, traveller, adventurer, humanitarian and street photographer,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://atodmagazine.com/interview/michal-josephy/|title=Michal Josephy - ATOD Magazine: A Taste of Dawn|publisher=Atodmagazine.com|accessdate=13 October 2014|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141025153207/http://atodmagazine.com/interview/michal-josephy/|archivedate=25 October 2014}}</ref> travel and science journalist and lecturer of [[science journalism]] and [[visual anthropology]] at Faculty of Humanities, [[Charles University]], Prague.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://is.cuni.cz/studium/predmety/index.php?do=ucit&fak=11240#24-sm|title=Předměty|publisher=Is.cuni.cz|accessdate=13 October 2014}}</ref>

== Biography ==
Michal Josephy is the grandson of [[Karel Josephy]], the [[Chief executive officer]] of Tobacco Factory<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://sechtl-vosecek.ucw.cz/en/cml/desky/deska0531.html|title=Šechtl & Voseček photo: Tobacco factory, 1888 (1890) {{!}} photo id: desky/deska0531|last=Jan|first=Hubička|last2=Marie|first2=Šechtlová|newspaper=Šechtl & Voseček|language=en-US|access-date=2016-11-09}}</ref> in [[Tábor]], [[Bohemia]]. This factory, built during [[Alexander Seik]] ´s mayorship, was the largest industrial enterprise in [[South Bohemian Region]] (1888 - 1953), one of the largest in [[Austria-Hungary|Austro-Hungarian Empire]], and the division of [[Tobacco industry]]  where women workers were exclusively employed.[http://socserv2.socsci.mcmaster.ca/econ/ugcm/3ll3/abbott/WomenIndustry.pdf]  He is a patrilinear descent of Anton Josephy, the city and criminal council <ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/?id=lZljAAAAcAAJ&pg=PA239&lpg=PA239&dq=anton+josephy#v=onepage&q=anton%20josephy&f=false|title=Kaiserlich königlicher Schematismus für das Königreich Böheim auf das gemeine Jahr ...|date=1827-01-01|publisher=Schönfeld|language=de}}</ref> and Bernart Yozeff, the owner and builder of the oldest surviving and exceptionally valuable inscription of mill establishing in [[Bohemia]] which is engraved in sandstone, late renaissance portal (1541)[http://web2.knihovna-cr.cz/digit/soupis/soupisvy/normal/0155.jpg]{{Dead link|date=April 2020 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}.

== Travels ==
In 2017 Josephy visited more than 100 countries and passed the requirements to join the prestigious [[Travelers' Century Club]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://travelerscenturyclub.org/about/history|title=TCC History {{!}} The Travelers' Century Club|website=travelerscenturyclub.org|language=en-US|access-date=2017-12-27}}</ref> He travelled through the most parts of [[Portuguese Empire]] from [[Brazil]], [[Goa]], [[Macau]], [[Malacca City]] to [[Japan]] and in the footsteps of French poet, adventurer and merchant [[Arthur Rimbaud]] from his birth - place [[Charleville-Mézières]] to [[Harar]] in [[Ethiopia]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cotmedia.cz/travel-service-magazine/#online|title=MyWings ČJ/AJ|last=s.r.o.|first=World Media Partners|website=www.cotmedia.cz|language=cs|access-date=2017-12-27}}</ref>
