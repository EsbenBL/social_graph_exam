'''Vusal Afras ogly Gasimli''' ({{lang-az|Vüsal Əfras oğlu Qasımlı}}) is an [[Azerbaijanis|Azerbaijani]] [[economist]] serving as the [[Center for Economic Reforms Analysis and Communication|Executive director of the Center for Economic Reforms Analysis and Communication of the Republic of Azerbaijan]] (since 2016),<ref name="ED" /> was the Head of Economic Analysis and Global Affairs Department of the [[Center for Strategic Studies under the President of Azerbaijan]] (2011-2016), and the President of [[ANS Group of Companies|"ANS-PRESS" Publishing House]] (2005-2007). Since 2021 he was appointed Focal Point of UNESCAP. He is the author of more than 10 books and 500 scientific works on economics in Azerbaijani, Russian, English, and Korean.<ref name="azrank" />

==Biography==
Vusal Gasimli was born in Imishli on 7 April 1975. He is married, with three children.<ref name="azrank">{{Cite web|url=https://azranking.az/employee/Vüsal%20Qasımlı-77.html|title=Bioqrafiya - Vüsal Əfras oğlu Qasımlı|access-date=2020-08-01|website=azranking.az|archive-url=http://archive.is/MTxqc|archive-date=2020-08-01|language=Az}}</ref>
=== Education ===
Gasimli received his primary and secondary education in high school #34 of Imishli. He entered the [[Azerbaijan State University of Economics]] in 1993 with the highest score. After graduation, Gasimli continued his education at [[Georgian Institute of Public Affairs]] in [[Tbilisi]] based on the study program of [[Louisiana State University]]<ref name="j">{{Cite web|url=https://jamestown.org/event/azerbaijan-georgia-turkey-foundations-and-challenges-of-an-emergent-strategic-alliance/|title=Azerbaijan, Georgia, Turkey: Foundations and Challenges of an Emergent Strategic Alliance|date=2013-04-30|access-date=2020-08-02|website=jamestown.org|archive-url=http://archive.is/fkQy9|archive-date=2020-08-02|language=en}}</ref> and received a master's degree in Journalism and Media Management in 2004.

In 2021 Vusal Gasimli was awarded a certificate of completion for the "Leading Economic Growth" Program, [February-May/2021], John F. Kennedy School of Government at Harvard University, Executive Education.  In 2013 Vusal Gasimli was given the title of associate professor and in 2021 he was given the title of professor.

Afterwards in 2009 Gasimli was awarded the degree of PhD on Economics,<ref name="turkey" /> and in 2017 he was awarded the title of Doctor of Science on Economics. Being a results-driven researcher with a strong background in [[macroeconomics]], his studies are focused on [[economic development]], [[Competition (economics)|competitiveness]], modernization, and [[geoeconomics]].

He is fluent in English, Turkish and Russian. Gasimli has an elementary knowledge of Spanish.<ref name="turkey" />

== Scientific activity == 
In 2010-2014, Gasimli was engaged in pedagogical activities at the [[Azerbaijan State University of Economics|UNEC]] Special Talent Group and MBA program. He delivered [[public lecture]]s at the [[Washington University in St. Louis]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://source.wustl.edu/2012/01/cniss-announces-spring-lecture-series/?fbclid=IwAR3ASlMIAWmGTUHjCgWo5IyQzoQi0Z-e7b_VZE66Sk-YvGBsTpdWOFguzDg|title=CNISS announces spring lecture series|date=2012-01-27|access-date=2020-08-04|website=source.wustl.edu|last=Schoenherr|first=Neil|archive-url=http://archive.is/sRpeH|archive-date=2020-08-04}}</ref> [[University of California]], [[The American University in Cairo]], [[University of Warsaw]], [[Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore]],<ref name="j" /> [[Hanyang University]], [[Hankuk University of Foreign Studies]], and [[Rio de Janeiro State University]]. In 2013, he was also employed by [[Korea Institute for International Economic Policy|KIEP]] as a visiting professor.

In 2012, Gasimli received the award of [[Astana Economic Forum]] for his outstanding research achievements.

His book, ''Geoeconomics'',<ref>{{Cite book|title=Geo-economics|last=Gasimli|first=Vusal|publisher=[[Center for Strategic Studies under the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan]]|year=2015|location=[[Baku]]|url=https://catalog.lib.uchicago.edu/vufind/Record/10513484/Holdings#tabnav|language=En|access-date=2020-08-02|archive-url=http://archive.is/6pRNZ|archive-date=2020-08-02}}</ref> a work highly estimated by the world scientific society, was published in 2015. Commenting on the book, the professor of [[King's College London]], [[Friedbert Pflüger]] noted that "Gasimli created a unique concept of [[geoeconomics]]".

== Career ==
From 2005 to 2007 Gasimli was the President of [[ANS Group of Companies|"ANS-PRESS" Publishing House]].<ref name="turkey">{{Cite web|url=http://www.antalyasummit.t20turkey.org/participants/vusal-gasimli-36|title=Antalya Summit T20 Turkey participants|date=2015-11-11|access-date=2020-08-02|website=antalyasummit.t20turkey.org|archive-url=http://archive.is/cZYRK|archive-date=2020-08-02|language=en}}</ref> In 2007-2011, he consulted on the projects of [[The European Union]], [[World Bank]], [[JICA]], and other intergovernmental organizations. In 2011, he was appointed the Head of Economic Analysis and Global Affairs Department of the [[Center for Strategic Studies under the President of Azerbaijan]]. He participated in the sessions of the [[UNIDO]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cmi.no/news/1279-diversifying-to-avoid-the-resource-curse|title=Diversifying to avoid the resource curse?|date=2013-12-20|access-date=2020-08-02|website=cmi.no|archive-url=http://archive.is/MxLEC|archive-date=2020-08-02|language=en}}</ref> Gasimli joined the T20 network (Think Tanks from [[G20]] countries) as a representative of [[Azerbaijan]] in 2015. 

In 2016, he became an economic development consultant for the [[Asian Development Bank]]. According to the final decision of the meeting held by the Supervisory Board of the Center for Economic Reforms Analysis and Communication, on May 2, 2016, Vusal Gasimli was appointed the [[Center for Economic Reforms Analysis and Communication|Executive director of the Center]].<ref name="ED">{{Cite web|url=https://iqtisadiislahat.org/pages/icraci_direktor-9|title=İcraçı direktor|access-date=2020-08-01|website=iqtisadiislahat.org|archive-url=http://archive.is/7xkY0|archive-date=2020-08-01|language=Az}}</ref> 

He is also a member of the government commission established on the basis of the Order of the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan dated July 13, 2016 No. 2199 "On additional measures to improve the business environment in the Republic of Azerbaijan and further improvement of the position of our country in international rankings".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.e-qanun.az/framework/33308|title=Azərbaycan Respublikasında biznes mühitinin əlverişliliyinin artırılması və beynəlxalq reytinqlərdə ölkəmizin mövqeyinin daha da yaxşılaşdırılması ilə bağlı əlavə tədbirlər haqqında Azərbaycan Respublikası Prezidentinin sərəncamı|date=2016-07-13|access-date=2020-08-01|website=e-qanun.az|archive-url=http://archive.is/ggEyc#selection-115.0-129.47|archive-date=2020-08-01|language=Az}}</ref> For his achievements in this field, in 2018, [[Ilham Aliyev]] awarded Gasimli the [[Taraggi|Taraggi Medal]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.e-qanun.az/framework/40616|title=Azərbaycan Respublikasında biznes mühitinin əlverişliliyinin artırılması və beynəlxalq reytinqlərdə ölkəmizin mövqeyinin daha da yaxşılaşdırılması sahəsində fərqlənən şəxslərin təltif edilməsi haqqında Azərbaycan Respublikası Prezidentinin sərəncamı|date=2018-11-12|access-date=2020-08-01|website=e-qanun.az|archive-url=http://archive.is/Rc0Os|archive-date=2020-08-01|language=Az}}</ref>
