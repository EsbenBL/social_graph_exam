'''David J. Jackson''' is a political science professor at [[Bowling Green State University]] in [[Ohio]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bgsu.edu/arts-and-sciences/political-science/faculty-and-staff-directory/David-Jackson.html|title=David Jackson, Ph.D.|website=Bowling Green State University|accessdate=19 October 2017}}</ref>  He specializes in the relationships between entertainment and politics.  He holds a BA from the University of Detroit, an MA from BGSU, and a Ph.D. from [[Wayne State University]].
