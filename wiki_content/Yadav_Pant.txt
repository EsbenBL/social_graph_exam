'''Yadav Prasad Pant''' ({{lang-ne|यादव प्रसाद पन्त}}) (1928<ref name="nanda">Nanda R. Shrestha ''“[https://books.google.com/books?id=qZapDQAAQBAJ&pg=PA292 Historical Dictionary of Nepal],”'' [[Rowman & Littlefield]], 2017, p.292</ref> - November 14, 2007) was a prominent [[Nepal]]ese economist and politician. He served as Fifth Governor of [[Nepal Rastra Bank]] between <ref name="nrb">http://www.nrb.org.np/aboutus/intro.php</ref> Throughout his career he held several high level positions including senior economist at the [[United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific|UN ESCAP]] in [[Bangkok]], [[Thailand]]; Governor of the [[Nepal Rastra Bank]] (1968–1973); Ambassador to [[Japan]] (1975–1979); and Minister for Commerce, Supplies, [[Ministry of Finance (Nepal)|Finance]] (1980–1983) and Water Resources (1986–1988).<ref name="nanda"/> He died at the age of 82 in [[Bumrungrad International|Bumrungrad Hospital]] in Thailand.<ref name="RN">{{cite web|url=http://www.gorkhapatra.org.np/content.php?nid=30228|title=Economist Yadav Pant passes away|access-date=2007-11-15|publisher=The Rising Nepal|archive-url=https://archive.is/20070726190528/http://www.gorkhapatra.org.np/content.php?nid=30228|archive-date=2007-07-26|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Panta has introduced the Nepalese economy to the world by his writings.<ref name="myrepublica">My Republica {{cite web |url= http://archives.myrepublica.com/portal/index.php?action=news_details&news_id=25302| title=Dr Yadav Prasad Pant A true hero| access-date= 1 October 2015}}</ref>
