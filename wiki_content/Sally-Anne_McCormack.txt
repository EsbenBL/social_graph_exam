'''Sally-Anne McCormack''' (born 12 March 1965) is an [[Australians|Australian]] clinical psychologist, former teacher and media commentator.

== Education ==
McCormack graduated from [[Monash University]] with a Masters in Psychology, as well as both ACU and [[Deakin University]] with teaching qualifications.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.healthshare.com.au/profile/professional/119236-sally-anne-mccormack/|title=Sally-Anne McCormack|website=healthshare.com.au|access-date=2019-05-30}}</ref> She was endorsed as a [[Clinical psychology|Clinical Psychologist]] in March 2010.<ref>https://www.nbcc.org/Assets/Newsletter/Issues/spring10.pdf}</ref>

== Career ==
McCormack filmed ''The Sally-Anne Show'' after creating TV pilots in both 2013 and 2014 for community television.  An article was written describing her varied career roles in [[The Sydney Morning Herald]] newspaper in 2015<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.smh.com.au/business/mind-matters-in-all-walks-of-life-20150312-1427il.html|title=Mind matters in all walks of life|last=Jennings|first=Josh|date=2015-03-13|website=The Sydney Morning Herald|language=en|access-date=2019-05-30}}</ref>

McCormack is a consulting [[psychologist]] for ''Mother & Baby'' magazine.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nowtolove.com.au/tags/Toddler|title=Toddler|website=Now To Love|language=en|access-date=2020-01-23}}</ref> She is the founder of ANTSA Psychology ("Automatic Negative Thoughts - Strategies for All") and is the author of ''Stomp Out The ANTs - Automatic Negative Thoughts'' as well as ''Living With ANTs''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sally-annemccormack.com.au/|title=Sally-anne McCormack Home|website=www.sally-annemccormack.com.au|access-date=2020-01-23}}</ref> In addition, she has created a poster titled "''Which ANT Are You?''" for children.

== Family ==
McCormack is a mother to four daughters; she lost a fifth daughter to [[Edwards syndrome]] in 1991.<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://catalogue.nla.gov.au/Record/4853558?lookfor=subject:Self-efficacy.&offset=9&max=10|title=Stomp out the ANTs|last=McCormack|first=Sally-Anne|last2=Smetham|first2=Michael|date=2009|publisher=Sally-Anne McCormack|isbn=978-0-9806867-0-8|location=Blackburn South, Vic}}</ref>
