'''Kevin James Murphy''' (born 1957) is a [[professor]] at the [[University of Southern California]]. Since 2006, Murphy has held the Kenneth L. Trefftzs Chair in Finance at the USC Marshall School of Business.<ref>https://msbfile03.usc.edu/digitalmeasures/kjmurphy/pci/KJMVita%201-2015-1.pdf</ref><ref>Marshall Faculty Directory - Kevin Murphy, "[http://marshallapps.usc.edu/portal/subapps/digitalmeasures/faculty.jsp?surveyId=48714] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110820171005/http://marshallapps.usc.edu/portal/subapps/digitalmeasures/faculty.jsp?surveyId=48714 |date=2011-08-20 }}", accessed 11/22/10</ref>  He is also a Professor of Law at the USC Gould School of Law<ref>Kevin Murphy, 03/29/10, "[http://lawweb.usc.edu/who/faculty/directory/contactInfo.cfm?detailID=239]"</ref> and Professor of Economics at USC's College of Letters, Arts & Science.<ref>USC Marshall - Corporate Governance Summit 2008, "[http://www.marshall.usc.edu/execed/programs/leadership/corporate-governance-summit-2008.htm#Murphy]"</ref>

== Career ==
Before teaching at USC, Kevin Murphy was a professor at the [[Harvard Business School]] and the [[University of Rochester]]'s [[William E. Simon Graduate School of Business Administration]].

The Institute for Scientific Information's Web of Knowledge compiles a Highly Cited list composed of the most highly cited individuals within each of 21 broad subject categories; Kevin J. Murphy has been listed under Economics/Business since at least 2003.<ref>ISI Web of Knowledge/ISIHighlyCited.com, "[http://hcr3.isiknowledge.com/author.cgi?&link1=Search&link2=Search%20Results&AuthLastName=murphy&AuthFirstName=kevin&AuthMiddleName=&AuthMailnstName=&CountryID=-1&DisciplineID=0&id=2677], accessed 8 April 2011."</ref>

Kevin Murphy is a member of the [[American Economic Association]] and an associate editor of the [[Journal of Corporate Finance]] and [[Journal of Financial Economics]].  He received his PhD in Economics from the [[University of Chicago]] in 1984 and his BA, summa cum laude, from [[UCLA]] in 1979.
