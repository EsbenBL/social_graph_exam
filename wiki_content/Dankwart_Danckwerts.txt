'''Dankwart Danckwerts''' (14 March 1933; [[Hamburg]] – 11 May 2012; Hamburg) was a German [[sociologist]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.uni-due.de/apps/rss.php?id=224&db=geswi&height=480&width=640 |title=Nachruf auf Dankwart Danckwerts |publisher=Uni-due.de |date= |accessdate=2012-06-16}}</ref>

After some time in business he qualified in ([[Sociology]] and [[Economics]]), graduated at the Hamburg University, 1960, and worked after that at the [[University of Münster]] with [[Helmut Schelsky]], where he gained his doctoral degree (''Dr. sc. pol.''), 1963.

After doing field work in [[Chile]], he was called to a chair of Sociology at the [[University of Duisburg]] up to 1998, where he, besides in general theory,  specialized in sociology of transportation. He remained active in sociological research.<ref>{{cite web|title=Prof. Dr. Dankwart Danckwerts |publisher=University of Duisburg-Essen |url=http://www.uni-due.de/soziologie/danckwerts_forschung.php |date=2010-03-12 |accessdate=2011-01-08 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110716201951/http://www.uni-due.de/soziologie/danckwerts_forschung.php |archivedate=2011-07-16 }}</ref>

A [[Festschrift]] was published on his 65th birthday in 1998.<ref>{{cite web|title=Soziologie als Krisenwissenschaft |publisher=LIT Verlag | url =http://www.lit-verlag.de/isbn/3-8258-3676-2 |accessdate =2011-01-08}}</ref>
