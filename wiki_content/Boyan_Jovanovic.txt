'''Boyan Jovanovic''' is a [[professor]] of [[economics]] at [[New York University]].

Jovanovic, of [[Serbs|Serbian]] descent, received his Bachelor's and master's degrees from the [[London School of Economics]] and his Ph.D. in Economics from the [[University of Chicago]].

Boyan Jovanovic is a Fellow of the [[American Academy of Arts and Sciences]] and the [[Econometric Society]]. He has won the 2019 Global Award for Entrepreneurship Research.
