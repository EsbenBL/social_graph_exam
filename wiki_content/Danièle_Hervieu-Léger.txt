'''Danièle Hervieu-Léger''' (born 3 February 1947) is a [[France|French]] sociologist specialized in the [[sociology of religion]].

==Biography==
A graduate of the [[Institut d'Études Politiques de Paris]] with a doctorate in sociology, she is also editor of the journal ''Archives des sciences sociales de la religion''.

She was a researcher at the [[National Center for Scientific Research]] (CNRS) from 1974 to 1993.<ref name="Alliance">{{cite web|title=Danièle Hervieu-Léger |url=http://www.alliance-us.org/fr/Page.Culture.Lecture.Hervieu.aspx |archive-url=https://archive.is/20130223122615/http://www.alliance-us.org/fr/Page.Culture.Lecture.Hervieu.aspx |url-status=dead |archive-date=23 February 2013 |publisher=Alliance Française USA |language=French |accessdate=20 August 2010 }}</ref>

As president of the [[École des hautes études en sciences sociales]] (EHESS) from March 2004 to March 2009, she undertook significant changes in the institution, particularly lowering the level of students before admission to the Master to abandon school diploma for the benefit of aligning the university system of [[:fr:Réforme LMD|LMD]]. She has also initiated a relocation of the school outside Paris, in [[Seine-Saint-Denis]], to integrate it into a new academic center for education, now called "Campus Condorcet", whose composition is constantly changing.

In late 2008, Hervieu-Léger was appointed by the Minister of Research, [[Valérie Pécresse]], Chair of the Steering Committee for developing the national strategy of research and innovation,<ref>{{cite web |title=Danièle Hervieu-Léger (EHESS) nommée présidente du comité de pilotage de l'élaboration de la stratégie nationale de recherche et d'innovation |url=http://www.fabula.org/actualites/article26142.php |date=14 October 2008 |publisher=Fabula, la recherche en littérature |first=Marc |last=Escola |language=French |accessdate=20 August 2010}}</ref> and resigned in 2009.<ref>{{cite web |title=Danièle Hervieu-Léger démissionne du comité de la recherche et de l'innovation |url=http://www.arhv.lhivic.org/index.php/2009/04/01/963-daniele-hervieu-leger-demissionne-du-comite-de-la-recherche-et-de-l-innovation |first=André |last=Gunthert |date=April 2009 |publisher=Recherche en histoire visuelle |language=French |accessdate=20 August 2010}}</ref>

Among her awards, she received the [[CNRS Silver medal]] in 2001, was named Knight of the Ordre National de la [[Légion d'Honneur]] and Officer of the National Order of Merit and the Academic Palms.<ref name="Alliance"/> In 2002 Hervieu-Léger received an [[Honorary degree|honorary doctorate]] from the Faculty of Theology at [[Uppsala University]], [[Sweden]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.uu.se/en/about-uu/traditions/prizes/honorary-doctorates/theology/|title=Honorary Doctors of the Faculty of Theology - Uppsala University, Sweden|website=www.uu.se|language=sv|access-date=2017-02-17}}</ref>

She wrote a number of books and articles, notably on the [[Catholic Church]]<ref>{{cite journal|title=Catholicisme, la fin d'un monde |url=http://www.esprit-et-vie.com/article.php3?id_article=656 |journal=Esprit & Vie |first=Robert |last=Pousseur |date=November 2003 |volume=94 |pages=27–29 |language=French |accessdate=20 August 2010 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101124041640/http://esprit-et-vie.com/article.php3?id_article=656 |archivedate=24 November 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |title=Danièle Hervieu-Léger: "Aujourd'hui, chacun bricole sa propre spiritualité" |url=http://www.lespectacledumonde.fr/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=285:dossier566&catid=29:dossier&Itemid=55 |journal=Le Monde du Spectacle |date=April 2010 |language=French |accessdate=20 August 2010}}</ref> and on [[cult]]s.<ref>{{cite web|title=Intervention de Danielle Hervieu Léger, présidente de l'EHESS |url=http://www.miviludes.gouv.fr/Intervention-de-Danielle-Hervieu?iddiv= |publisher=[[MIVILUDES]] |language=French |accessdate=20 August 2010 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110927153525/http://www.miviludes.gouv.fr/Intervention-de-Danielle-Hervieu?iddiv= |archivedate=27 September 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Danièle Hervieu-Léger, 2001, ''La religion en miettes ou la question des sectes'', coll.
