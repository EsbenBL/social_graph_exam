'''Frank-Jürgen Richter''' (born 1967) is a German entrepreneur, economic advisor, and commentator. He is best known as the chairman of [[Horasis]] and founder of the [[Horasis Global Meeting]], as well as a former director of the [[World Economic Forum]].

==Early life==
After graduating with a degree in [[industrial engineering]] from the [[Karlsruhe Institute of Technology|University of Karlsruhe]] (now part of the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology) and academic stays in France and Mexico, Richter pursued his PhD-studies at the [[University of Tsukuba]] and obtained his doctoral degree from the [[University of Stuttgart]].

From 1996 until 2000, he lived and worked in Asia where he developed and managed a European Multinationals' Asian operations, and then became a Director of the [[World Economic Forum]] from 2001 to 2004.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.worldtradereview.com/news.asp?pType=N&iType=A&iID=70&siD=11&nID=11832 |title=The World Trade Review |publisher=The World Trade Review |date=15 November 2003 |accessdate=2014-08-14 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140814170127/http://www.worldtradereview.com/news.asp?pType=N&iType=A&iID=70&siD=11&nID=11832 |archive-date=14 August 2014 |url-status=dead }}</ref> Following the incorporation of Horasis in 2005 he has been a frequent international traveler, researcher and commentator as well as leading the annual Business Meetings.

==Career==
In 2005, Richter founded [[Horasis]], an independent think tank for global business topics.<ref>{{cite web|author=Horasis.org |url=http://www.horasis.org/newsweek%20_1.php |title=News :: The Continued Rise of the Rest |publisher=Horasis.org |date=18 November 2009 |accessdate=2014-08-14}}</ref> Richter works with businesspeople, politicians and intellectuals.<ref>{{cite web|author=17. November 2009 |url=http://www.wiwo.de/politik-weltwirtschaft/platzt-in-china-die-naechste-blase-413871/ |title=Konjunktur: Platzt in China die nächsteBlase? – Ausland – Politik – Wirtschaftswoche |language=de |publisher=Wiwo.de |date= |accessdate=2014-08-14}}</ref><ref name="youtube1">{{cite web|author=Global Asia TV |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W0YGLwkWYlI |title=ArkadyDvorkovich with Frank-Jurgen Richter, Chairman, Horasis |publisher=YouTube |date= |accessdate=2014-08-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.keminet.net/~minfagov/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=7480:ministri-i-puneve-te-jashtme-z-edmond-haxhinasto-priti-ne-takim-presidentin-e-horasis-&catid=112:lajme&Itemid=88&lang=en |title=The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Edmond Haxhinasto Received the President of " Horasis" |publisher=Keminet.net |date= |accessdate=2014-08-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.khaleejtimes.com/DisplayArticle08.asp?xfile=data/opinion/2011/October/opinion_October142.xml&section=opinion |title=Small is also beautiful |publisher=Khaleejtimes.com |date=29 October 2011 |accessdate=2014-08-14}}</ref> He advises governments and private sector organizations on issues such as globalization, trade, and sustainable development.<ref name="youtube1"/><ref>{{cite web|author=Horasis.org |url=http://www.horasis.org/press_releases_all_21.php |title=News :: China-Europe Connector |publisher=Horasis.org |date=22 September 2007 |accessdate=2014-08-14}}</ref>

Richter is a Member of the Advisory Board on impact leadership of [[University of Cambridge]] and [[Cheung Kong Graduate School of Business]].<ref>[https://english.ckgsb.edu.cn/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Slide11-1.jpeg.webp CKGSB. Igniting Innovation for Impact program]</ref>

== Awards ==
