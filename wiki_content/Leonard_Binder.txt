'''Leonard Binder''' (1927-2015)<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2-BnA0y8xVMC&q=%22Binder,+Leonard%22+AND+%221927%22|title = Directory of Foreign Area Training Fellows, 1952-1959|last1 = Foundation|first1 = Ford|year = 1960}}</ref><ref>https://senate.universityofcalifornia.edu/in-memoriam/files/leonard-binder.html</ref> was an American political scientist. He was a [[distinguished professor]] of [[political science]] and the former director of the Near East Center at the [[University of California, Los Angeles]] (UCLA). Binder was also Chair of the Political Science Departments of UCLA and the [[University of Chicago]].<ref>[http://www.international.ucla.edu/person.asp?Facultystaff_ID=216 Faculty profile] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20020903102031/http://www.international.ucla.edu/person.asp?Facultystaff_ID=216 |date=2002-09-03 }}, UCLA International Institute, Retrieved 2010-07-14.</ref> He was elected a Fellow of the [[American Academy of Arts and Sciences]] in 2002<ref name=AAAS>{{cite web|title=Book of Members, 1780â€“2010: Chapter B|url=http://www.amacad.org/publications/BookofMembers/ChapterB.pdf|publisher=American Academy of Arts and Sciences|accessdate=July 20, 2011}}</ref>
