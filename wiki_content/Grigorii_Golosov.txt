'''Grigorii V. Golosov''' ({{lang-ru|Григорий Васильевич Голосов}}), sometimes spelled as '''Grigory Golosov''', is a [[Russia]]n [[political scientist]]. He is a professor and the Head of Political Science Department at [[European University at Saint Petersburg|the European University at Saint Petersburg]], Russia.<ref>{{cite web |title=Golosov, Grigorii |url=https://eusp.org/en/people/golosov |publisher=European University at St. Petersburg}}</ref> He is notable as expert on political institutions and electoral systems, in particular, in application to Russia.<ref name="eusp">{{cite web |title=Department of Political Science |url=https://eu.spb.ru/en/political-science/about/faculty/3542-golosov-3542 |publisher=European University at Saint Petersburg}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |last1=Ачкасов |first1=В.А. |title=Сравнительная политология : Курс лекций |date=2002 |publisher=Социологическое общество им. М.М. Ковалевского |location=Saint Petersburg |isbn=5-94348-013-7 |language=Russian}}</ref> His article "[http://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/0010414003256115 Electoral Systems and Party Formation in Russia]" (''Comparative Political Studies'', Vol. 36, No. 8, October 2003, pp. 912-935) received a [http://www.apsanet.org/STAFF/Membership-Workspace/Organized-Sections/Organized-Section-Awards/Organized-Section-Awards/Section-8#longley Lawrence Longley Award] of the American Political Science Association for the best journal article on electoral systems and representation published in 2003. Golosov is also a popular political analyst writing for media such as Forbes<ref>{{cite web |title=Григорий Голосов |url=https://www.forbes.ru/person/50773-golosov-grigorii |work=[[Forbes]] |language=Russian}}</ref> and polit.ru .<ref>{{cite web |title=Григорий Голосов |url=https://polit.ru/author/272817/ |publisher=[[polit.ru]]|language=Russian}}</ref>

==Biography==
Grigorii Golosov was born in 1963. After his doctoral studies, from 1987 to 1995, he worked at [[Novosibirsk State University]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Григорий Голосов |url=https://openuni.io/expert/24/ |publisher=Открытый Университет |language=Russian}}</ref>
