'''Aaron Victor Cicourel''', who is professor emeritus of sociology at the [[University of California, San Diego]], specializes in sociolinguistics,<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://www.sociologyguide.com/ethnomethodology/aaron-cicourel-cognitive.php | title=Aaron Cicourel's Cognitive Approach, Ethnomethodological Inquiries, Ethnomethodology, Sociology Guide}}</ref> medical communication, decision-making, and child socialization. Early in his career, he was intellectually influenced greatly by [[Alfred Schutz]], [[Erving Goffman]],<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.unlv.edu/centers/cdclv/archives/interactionism/goffman/cicourel_09.html |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2010-12-07 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110105061652/http://www.unlv.edu/centers/cdclv/archives/interactionism/goffman/cicourel_09.html |archivedate=2011-01-05 }}</ref> and [[Harold Garfinkel]].

==Career==

After receiving his B.A and M.A. from [[UCLA]] and his Ph.D. from [[Cornell University]], Cicourel accepted posts as visiting assistant professor at [[Northwestern University]], assistant professor to associate professor at the [[University of California, Riverside]],<ref>{{cite web |url=http://sociology.ucsd.edu/faculty/bio/cicourel.shtml |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2016-12-27 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100629211842/http://sociology.ucsd.edu/faculty/bio/cicourel.shtml |archivedate=2010-06-29 }}</ref>  visiting professor at the [[University of Buenos Aires]] in Argentina, lecturer in the Department of Sociology and associate research sociologist in the Center for the Study of Law and Society at the Universaity of California at Berkeley, visiting professor at [[Columbia University]], professor of sociology at the University of California at Berkeley, professor of sociology at the University of California at Santa Barbara, professor in the School of Medicine and Department of Sociology at the University of California at San Diego, and research professor of cognitive science at the latter institution.

Cicourel held a [[Russell Sage Foundation]] Post Doctoral Fellowship at the [[University of California at Los Angeles]] Medical Center, a  [[National Science Foundation]] Senior Postdoctoral Fellowship at [[London University]] in England, a [[Guggenheim Fellowship]] at the [[Autonomous University of Madrid|University of Madrid]] in Spain, and was a [[Fulbright]] lecturer in Brazil and Spain.  In November 2007, he was awarded Docteur Honoris Causa by [[University of Fribourg|Universit√© de Fribourg]] in Switzerland, and in 2008 was awarded Doctor Honoris Causa  [[Complutense University of Madrid]].

He was elected Fellow of the [[American Academy of Arts and Sciences]] and of the [[American Association for the Advancement of Science]]. Cicourel lives in [[Berkeley, California]].
