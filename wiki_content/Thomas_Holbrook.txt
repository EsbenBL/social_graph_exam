'''Thomas Holbrook''' (born 1949) is an Illinois politician who serves as the St. Clair County Clerk. He was appointed to replace Bob Delaney.<ref>http://www.bnd.com/news/local/article113690369.html</ref><ref>https://www.stltoday.com/news/local/govt-and-politics/st-clair-county-pays-to-settle-harassment-claims-against-ex/article_5025f6f1-217a-59d8-bb86-3b078e3f9e93.html</ref> Prior to this he served as Chairman of the [[Illinois Pollution Control Board]] from 2011 to 2013<ref>http://www.ipcb.state.il.us/AboutTheBoard/BoardMembers.asp</ref> and a [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democratic]] member of the [[Illinois House of Representatives]] representing the 113th District for eight terms from 1995 until 2011.
