'''Alfredo Castillero Hoyos''' is a [[Panamanian]] [[political scientist]], human rights [[activist]], and [[professor]]. He was a member of the [[United Nations]]'s [[Human Rights Committee]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.un.org/News/Press/docs/2005/hrct670.doc.htm|title=Experts to Review Reports of Canada , Paraguay , Italy , Brazil|date=2005-10-13|work=HR/CT/670|publisher=United Nations General Assembly|accessdate=2010-02-16}}</ref> and ran for the ombudsman office of [[Panama]].

Alfredo Castillero Hoyos graduated at the [[University of Warwick]] where he obtained a master's degree in political studied in 1993. He then went on to complete post-graduate studies in the methodology of social sciences and a doctorate in political studies, both at the [[University of Portsmouth]] in 1999.
