{{Short description|British political analyst and writer}}
'''Dennis Kavanagh''' (born 27 March 1941) is a [[United Kingdom|British]] political analyst and since 1996 has been Professor of Politics at the [[University of Liverpool]], and now Emeritus Professor. He has written extensively on post-[[World War II|war]] [[Politics of the United Kingdom|British politics]]. With [[David Butler (academic)|David Butler]], he wrote the series of books on [[British general elections]], such as ''The British General Election of 2010'',<ref>Kavanagh, Dennis and Cowley, Philip. ''The British General Election of 2010''. Palgrave Macmillan, 2010. {{ISBN|978-0-230-52190-2}}.</ref> and most recently, ''The British General Election of 2015''.<ref>{{cite book|author1= Dennis Kavanagh|author2= Philip Cowley|author2-link= Philip Cowley|title= The British General Election of 2015|year= 2016|isbn= 9781137366115|publisher= [[Palgrave Macmillan]]|location= London|doi= 10.1057/9781137366115|url= https://dspace.lboro.ac.uk/2134/21439}}</ref>
