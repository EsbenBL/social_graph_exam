'''William Dale Berry''' is the ''Syde P. Deeb Eminent Scholar in Political Science'' and ''[[Marian D. Irish]] Professor of Political Science'' at [[Florida State University]].  His research analyzes the impact of electoral competition on the policy choices made by [[State legislature (United States)|state legislator]]s, and the effect of state [[welfare]] policy on [[poverty in the United States]].  Berry's research on methodology focuses on the development of techniques for estimating [[econometric]] models with binary [[dependent variable]]s, and methods for studying policy diffusion using [[geographical information system]]s.
