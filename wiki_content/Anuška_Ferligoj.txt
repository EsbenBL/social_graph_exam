'''Anuška Ferligoj''' is a [[Slovenians|Slovenian]] [[mathematician]], born August 19, 1947 in [[Ljubljana]], [[Slovenia]], whose specialty is [[statistics]] and [[Social network analysis|network analysis]]. Her specific interests include [[multivariate analysis]] (theory and application in social sciences, medicine, etc.), [[cluster analysis]] (constraints, multi-criteria clustering), [[social network analysis]] (blockmodeling, reliability and [[validity (statistics)|validity]] of network measurement), methodological research of [[public opinion]], analysis of scientific networks. She is Fellow of the [[European Academy of Sociology]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://videolectures.net/icots2010_ferligoj_ugs/|title=Unifying graduate statistic: a big umbrella for a small country|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref>

She is a Professor Emeritus (2020) at the [[University of Ljubljana]], [[Slovenia]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/research/research-centres/department-of-sociology/centre-for-methodology-and-informatics/news/prof.-dr.-anu%C5%A1ka-ferligoj-awarded-with-the-title-professor-emeritus-of-university-of-ljubljana|title=Dr. Anuška Ferligoj awarded with the title Professor Emeritus of University of Ljubljana.|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref> and has been employed by the Faculty of Social Sciences since 1972.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/news-and-information/contacts/teachers/info/anuska-ferligoj#zivljenjepis|title=Dr. Anuška Ferligoj, Professor Emeritus. University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Social Sciences.|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref> In the 2003—2005, she was the dean of the [[Faculty of Social Sciences, Ljubljana|Faculty of Social Sciences]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/home|title=University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Social Sciences.|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref> In 1992 – 2012, she was the head of the Centre for Methodology and Informatics at the Institute of Social Sciences (currently – its associate member).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/research/research-centres/department-of-sociology/centre-for-methodology-and-informatics|title=Centre for Methodology and Informatics. University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Social Sciences.|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref> In 2002 – 2013, she headed the Graduate Program on Statistics,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://videolectures.net/icots2010_ferligoj_ugs/|title=Unifying graduate statistic: a big umbrella for a small country|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref> and in 2012 – 2020 – the Master program on Applied [[Statistics]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://fri.uni-lj.si/en/study-programme/applied-statistics|title=Applied Statistics. Masters programme. Anuška Ferligoj.|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref>

Sunbelt Archives.|access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Anuška Ferligoj obtained her B.S. in [[Mathematics]] and [[Physics]] in 1971, and her M.S. in [[Operational Research]] in 1979 from the [[University of Ljubljana]]. In 1983 she obtained her Ph.D. in [[Information Science]] from the same university under the supervision of Professor Branislav Ivanović.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://vladowiki.fmf.uni-lj.si/lib/exe/fetch.php?media=nusa:pdf:cv_aferligoj.pdf|title=Anuška Ferligoj. European Curriculum Vitae.|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/news-and-information/contacts/teachers/info/anuska-ferligoj#zivljenjepis|title=Dr. Anuška Ferligoj, Professor Emeritus. University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Social Sciences|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref>

Since 1972, she was employed as [[Teaching assistant|Assistant]], [[Assistant Professor]], [[Associate Professor]] and finally from 1994 as Full [[Professor]] at the Faculty of Social Sciences.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/news-and-information/contacts/teachers/info/anuska-ferligoj#zivljenjepis|title=Dr. Anuška Ferligoj, Professor Emeritus. University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Social Sciences|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref>

==Research and career==
In 1990—1991, Anuška Ferligoj received Fulbright Award, visiting University of Pittsburgh. There she worked with Professor [[Patrick Doreian]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://patrickdoreian.com/research/|title=Patrick Doreian’s blog.|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref> on research on connecting cluster and network analysis.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://libraries.uark.edu/specialcollections/fulbrightdirectories/MC703_CIES_Box91_1990-91_Volume_1.pdf|title=Fulbright Scholars Directory. 1990-1991, Volume 1, p.80.|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref>

In the 2003—2005, Anuška Ferligoj was the first female dean of the Faculty of Social Sciences.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/about-us/gallery/faculty-art-collection|title=University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Social Sciences. Faculty art collection.|access-date=22 August 2021}}</ref> She organized the Centre of Methodology and Informatics at the Institute of Social Sciences (Faculty of Social Sciences), in 1992 and led it until 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/research/research-centres/department-of-sociology/centre-for-methodology-and-informatics|title=Centre for Methodology and Informatics. University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Social Sciences|access-date=22 August 2021}}</ref> She initiated the Graduate Program on Statistics <ref>{{cite web|url=http://videolectures.net/icots2010_ferligoj_ugs/|title=Unifying graduate statistic: a big umbrella for a small country|access-date=22 August 2021}}</ref> in 2002, heading it until 2013 and the Master program on Applied Statistics <ref>{{cite web|url=http://vladowiki.fmf.uni-lj.si/lib/exe/fetch.php?media=nusa:pdf:cv_aferligoj.pdf|title=Applied Statistics. Masters programme. University of Ljubljana.|access-date=22 August 2021}}</ref> in 2012, heading it up to 2020. Since 2017, she is the academic supervisor of the International Laboratory for Applied Network Research of National Research University Higher School of Economics in Moscow, Russia.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://fri.uni-lj.si/en/study-programme/applied-statistics|title=Higher School of Economics. Anuška Ferligoj|access-date=22 August 2021}}</ref>

Anuška Ferligoj is a fellow of the European Academy of Sociology<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.european-academy-sociology.eu/fellows/ferligoj.html|title=European Academy of Sociology – Fellows. Prof. Dr. Anuška Ferligoj.|access-date=22 August 2021}}</ref> and elected member of the International Statistical Institute.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.isi-web.org/about-isi/who-is-isi/members/indivual|title=International Statistical Institute. Individual Members.|access-date=22 August 2021}}</ref> She has been a member of boards of the International Network for Social Network Analysis, International Federation of Classification Societies, and Research Committee RC33 on Logic and Methodology at the International Sociological Association.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://vladowiki.fmf.uni-lj.si/lib/exe/fetch.php?media=nusa:pdf:cv_aferligoj.pdf|title=Anuška Ferligoj. European Curriculum Vitae.|access-date=22 August 2021}}</ref> She was a member of other international and domestic societies and associations, including Classification Society of North America, [[Psychometric Society]]. She is a member of the [[Mathematical]], Physical and Astronomical Society of Slovenia, Statistical Society of Slovenia (being its chair in 1982 – 1983),<ref>{{cite web|url=https://stat-d.si/home/organi-drustva/|title= Statistično društvo Slovenije. Anuška Ferligoj was the head of the research program Social Science Methodology, Statistics and Informatics (1998-2019)<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/news-and-information/contacts/teachers/info/anuska-ferligoj#zivljenjepis|title= Dr. Anuška Ferligoj, Professor Emeritus. ==Academic service==
Anuška Ferligoj organized more than 30 international conferences, including the conferences sponsored by International Sociological Association's (ISA) research committee RC33 on Logic and Methodology (1988), International Network for Social Network Analysis - SUNBELT (2004), International Federation of Classification Societies - IFCS (2006), European Survey Research Association - ESRA (2013) and the first meeting of the COST Action 15109 (2016).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/news-and-information/contacts/teachers/info/anuska-ferligoj#zivljenjepis|title=Dr. Anuška Ferligoj, Professor Emeritus. University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Social Sciences|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref>

Anuška Ferligoj was founding editor of the journal Advances in Methodology and Statistics (Metodološki zvezki) in 1987 – 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://stat-d.si/mz/|title=Metodološki zvezki – Advances in Methodology and Statistics. Editorial board. Founding Editors|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref> Currently, she is a member of the editorial board of this journal, and of the journals Methodology, Statistic in Transition, Bulletin of Sociological Methodology, Corvinus Journal of Sociology and Social Policy, and Bilten Statističnega društva Slovenije (being its editor in 1982 – 1983). She was  a member of the editorial boards of Structure and Dynamics: eJournal of Anthropology and Related Sciences, Journal of Classification, Journal of Mathematical Sociology, Social Networks, Statistical Analysis and Data Mining, Statistical Theory & Method Abstracts, Advances in Data Analysis and Classification, IFCS Newsletter, Statistička revija.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://vladowiki.fmf.uni-lj.si/lib/exe/fetch.php?media=nusa:pdf:cv_aferligoj.pdf|title=Anuška Ferligoj. European Curriculum Vitae.|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fdv.uni-lj.si/en/news-and-information/contacts/teachers/info/anuska-ferligoj#zivljenjepis|title=Dr. Anuška Ferligoj, Professor Emeritus. University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Social Sciences|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref> She also edited a special double issue of the journal Quality & Quantity (1989)<ref>{{cite web|url=https://link.springer.com/journal/11135/volumes-and-issues/23-3/|title=Quality & Quantity. International Journal of Methodology. Volume 23, issue 3-4, September 1989|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref> and a special issue of the journal Advances in Data Analysis and Classification (2011).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://link.springer.com/journal/11634/volumes-and-issues/5-2/|title=Advances in Data Analysis and Classification. Volume 5, issue 2, July 2011|access-date=23 August 2021}}</ref> She edited several proceedings of papers presented at the international scientific conferences on statistics and methodology that she organized.

Anuška Ferligoj was a visiting professor at the University of Pittsburgh (1996) and at the University of Vienna (2009/10). She took part in Invited Colloquia at the universities and institutes in Austria, Belgium, Croatia, France, Germany, Hungary, Italy, the Netherlands, Russia, Serbia, Slovakia, Spain, and USA.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://vladowiki.fmf.uni-lj.si/lib/exe/fetch.php?media=nusa:pdf:cv_aferligoj.pdf|title=Anuška Ferligoj.
