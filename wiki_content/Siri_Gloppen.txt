'''Siri Gloppen'''  (born 30 March 1964) is a Norwegian political scientist who currently serves as professor of Comparative Politics at the University of Bergen (UiB), Senior Researcher at the [[Chr. Michelsen Institute]] for Research on Development and Justice (CMI), and Director of the CMI-[[University of Bergen|UiB]] Center on Law and Social Transformation.<ref>[http://www.vg.no/nyheter/utenriks/soer-afrika/soer-afrika-setter-inn-haeren-for-aa-beskytte-innvandrere/a/23437435/ Sør-Afrika setter inn hæren for å beskytte innvandrere]</ref>

Gloppen has been research coordinator at [[PluriCourts - Centre for the study of the Legitimate Roles of the Judiciary in the Global Order|PluriCourts]], [[University of Oslo]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.jus.uio.no/pluricourts/english/publications/2015/2015-02-11-gloppen-corruption.html|title=Courts, corruption and {{sic|judical|nolink=y}} independence - PluriCourts - Centre for the Study of the Legitimate Roles of the Judiciary in the Global Order|website=www.jus.uio.no|language=en|access-date=2019-03-10|last3=fax}}</ref> and Scientific Director for the Bergen Summer Research School (BSRS) 2014. She is also affiliated with the [[Centre for Policy Research]] in [[New Delhi]].

Gloppen is a board member of the Rafto Foundation for Human Rights since 2002 and was head of the Rafto Prize Committee 2010-2011.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rafto.no/about-us/contact/the-board-of-directors|title=The Board of Directors|website=The Rafto Foundation|access-date=2019-03-10}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://humanrightshouse.org/articles/siri-gloppen-new-head-of-the-rafto-prize-committee/|title=Siri Gloppen new Head of the Rafto Prize Committee|date=2010-02-25|website=Human Rights House Foundation|language=en-US|access-date=2019-03-10}}</ref> Since 2019, she has been a member of the ''[[The Lancet|Lancet]]''–SIGHT Commission on Peaceful Societies Through Health and Gender Equality, chaired by [[Tarja Halonen]].<ref>[https://sight.nu/commissioners/ Commissioners] ''[[The Lancet|Lancet]]''–SIGHT Commission on Peaceful Societies Through Health and Gender Equality.</ref>
