'''David H. Bayley''' (March 26, 1933 – May 10, 2020) was an American political scientist who taught at the [[University of Denver]] and the [[State University of New York at Albany]].<ref name="mcpeekobit">{{cite web |title=Obituary: David H. Bayley |url=https://www.mcpeekfuneralhome.com/obituary/David-Bayley |website=McPeek-Hoekstra Funeral Home |accessdate=May 25, 2020|date=May 10, 2020}}</ref> He authored 18 books,<ref name="mcpeekobit"/> and he became a "policing research pioneer."<ref name="heslopcontribution">{{cite web |last1=Heslop |first1=Richard |title=The contribution of David H. Bayley, policing research pioneer |url=https://core.ac.uk/download/pdf/74204542.pdf |website=COnnecting REpositories |publisher=The Open University |accessdate=May 25, 2020}}</ref>
