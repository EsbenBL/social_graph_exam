'''Myriam Benraad''' is a [[French people|French]] [[political scientist]]. She specializes in the politics of the [[Arab world]].

==Career==
Benraad graduated from the [[Institut d'études politiques]] in 2002. She then received her PhD in political science from the same institution in 2011.<ref>{{cite book |author=Gilles Kepel |title=L'Irak au miroir de l'occupation, récit d'une déchirure identitaire: un examen critique de l'expérience collective arabe sunnite (2003-2009) |language=fr |location=Paris |editor=Institut d'études politiques de Paris |year=2011}}.</ref>

Benraad was an early scholar of the [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant]], chronicling its accumulation of power beginning in 2006 and its centrality in the evolution and reconfigurations of power that have occurred in [[Iraq]] since then.<ref>{{cite web |first=Jean-Dominique |last=Merchet |url=https://www.lopinion.fr/edition/international/en-irak-al-qaida-capitalise-mecontentement-sunnites-7868 |language=fr |title=" En Irak, "Al-Qaïda capitalise sur le mécontentement des sunnites" " |work=L'Opinion |date=6 January 2014 |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |first=Myriam |last=Benraad |language=fr |url=https://www.huffingtonpost.fr/myriam-benraad/fallouja-irak-sunnites_b_4549351.html |title= Révolte sunnite et impasse de la violence: l'Irak au bord de l'implosion ?  |work=Le Huffington Post |date=6 January 2014 |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref> Her doctoral thesis, defended in January 2011, examined the sociopolitical group experiences of [[Sunni Islam|Sunni]] Iraqi Arabs in the context of the [[History of Iraq (2003–2011)|American occupation of Iraq]].<ref>{{cite book |first=Myriam |last=Benraad |url=http://www.theses.fr/2011IEPP0069 |title=L'Irak au miroir de l'occupation : récit d'une déchirure identitaire : un examen critique de l'expérience collective arabe sunnite (2003-2009) thèse de doctorat en science politique soutenue |publisher=l'Institut d'études politiques de Paris (Aires politiques comparées, programme Monde musulman) |date=26 January 2011 |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref>

In 2013, she became a researcher at the Institut de Recherches et d'études sur le Monde Arabe et Musulman ([[:fr:Institut de recherches et d'études sur le monde arabe et musulman|fr]]).<ref name="huffi">{{cite web |url=https://www.huffingtonpost.fr/author/myriam-benraad/ |title=Myriam Benraad |language=fr |publisher=Huffington Post}}</ref>

In 2015, Benraad was among the three finalists for the Brienne Prize for Geopolitical Writing, given by the association Lire la Société together with the [[Ministry of Armed Forces (France)|French Ministry of Defense]], as well as for the Worlds at War, Worlds at Peace Award from the Historical Book Fair at [[Verdun]] for her work ''Irak : la revanche de l'Histoire. De l'occupation étrangère à l'État islamique'' (Iraq: History's revenge. From foreign occupation to the Islamic State).<ref>{{cite web |language=fr |url=http://rpdefense.over-blog.com/2015/09/remise-du-prix-brienne-du-livre-geopolitique.html |title=Remise du Prix Brienne du Livre Géopolitique |publisher=RP Défense |date=29 September 2015 |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.verdun-meuse.fr/images/pages/CommuniqueSalon2015.pdf |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151208013734/http://www.verdun-meuse.fr/images/pages/CommuniqueSalon2015.pdf |url-status=dead |archive-date=8 December 2015 |title=Prix Mondes en guerre, mondes en paix, 12e |publisher=Salon du Livre d'Histoire de Verdun, Centre mondial de la Paix, des libertés et des droits de l'Homme, Association 14/18 Meuse |date=8 November 2015 |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref> This work was also the subject of numerous reviews in the popular media.<ref>{{cite web |first=Gaïdz |last=Minassian |url=https://www.lemonde.fr/proche-orient/article/2015/02/19/le-livre-du-jour-l-orient-sunnite-complique_4579645_3218.html |title=Le livre du jour : l'Orient sunnite compliqué |language=fr |work=Le Monde |date=19 February 2015 |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |first=Éric |last=Chopin |url=https://www.ouest-france.fr/irak-le-chaos-est-le-produit-de-lhistoire-3227516 |title=Irak : " Le chaos est le produit de l'histoire " (entretien) |work=Ouest France |date=4 March 2015 |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref>

In 2017, she joined the faculty at [[Leiden University]] in [[Leiden]], [[Netherlands]].<ref name=huffi/> In 2019, she was a consultant to the [[Ministry of Europe and Foreign Affairs|French Ministry of Europe and Foreign Affairs]] and the French Ministry of Defense regarding the [[Investment in post-invasion Iraq|reconstruction of Iraq]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Enjeux et perspectives de la reconstruction de l'Irak - Sénat |url=https://www.senat.fr/les_actus_en_detail/article/enjeux-et-perspectives-de-la-reconstruction-de-lirak.html |language=fr |publisher=Senate of France |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref>

Benraad has been involved in professional service roles through the French Political Science Association ([[:fr:Association française de science politique|fr]]), the Middle East Studies Association, and the International Center for Counter-Terrorism.<ref>{{cite web |language=fr |title=Annuaire Association Française de Science Politique |url=https://www.afsp.info/association/annuaire/ |publisher=French Political Science Association |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Iremam - v_dev - BENRAAD Myriam |language=fr |url=http://iremam.preprod.lamp.cnrs.fr/spip.php?article1768&lang=fr |publisher=iremam |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Commission d'enquête sur l'organisation et les moyens des services de l'État pour faire face à l'évolution de la menace terroriste après la chute de l'État Islamique - Sénat |language=fr |url=https://www.senat.fr/commission/enquete/chute_etat_islamique/index.html |publisher=Senate of France |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref>

Benraad has also worked on the topics of [[cyberbullying]] and [[cyberstalking]], criticising governments for their lack of response to these issues as they grew in prominence, and describing the development of cyber-[[psychopathy|psychopaths]] as a new type of violence.<ref>{{cite web |language=fr |title=BLOG - Le cyberpsychopathe, un nouveau genre de tueur en série |url=https://www.huffingtonpost.fr/myriam-benraad/le-cyberpsychopathe-un-nouveau-genre-de-tueur-en-serie_a_23591689/ |publisher=Le Huffington Post |date=19 November 2018 |access-date=16 February 2020}}</ref>
