'''Stefan Czarnowski''' (1879 &ndash; 1937) was a [[Poland|Polish]] [[sociologist]], professor of the [[University of Warsaw]].

Member of the Polish pro-independence movements, he fought in the [[Polish Legions in World War I|Polish Legions]] and the [[Polish-Soviet War]]. At first supporter of [[endecja]], he gravitated towards supporting [[Polish Socialist Party]].

His works centered on sociology of culture and religion. He studied social movements, theory of heroes as well as history of the Celts.
