'''David Tzuriel''' ({{lang-he|דוד צוריאל}}) is an [[Israelis|Israeli]] clinical and educational [[psychologist]]. His expertise is [[dynamic assessment]]. Currently he works as a [[professor]] at [[Bar Ilan University]]. He was born on September 23, 1946.
