'''Peter K. Manning''' (born September 27, 1940) is an American [[sociologist]] who is an author and speaker on the topic of policing organizations.

== Background ==
Peter K. Manning was born in [[Salem, Oregon]] on September 27, 1940. He graduated from [[Willamette University]] in 1961. Manning went on to earn his [[Master of Arts|M.A.]] and [[Ph.D]] in [[Sociology]] from [[Duke University]] in 1963 and 1966 respectively.

== Career ==
Peter K. Manning holds the Brooks Chair in the College of [[Criminal Justice]] at [[Northeastern University]], [[Boston, MA]]. He has taught at [[Michigan State]], [[MIT]], [[Oxford]], and the [[University of Michigan]].<ref>
