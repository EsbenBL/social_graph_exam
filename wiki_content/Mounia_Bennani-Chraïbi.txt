'''Mounia Bennani-Chraïbi''' is a [[Moroccans|Moroccan]] [[Political science|political scientist]], author and professor at the [[University of Lausanne]]. She published several works on activism, social media, youth issues and elections.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://economia.ma/content/mounia-bennani-chraibi|title=Mounia Bennani-Chraibi|date=2015-07-15|website=Economia|language=FR|access-date=2019-10-05}}</ref>

== Biography ==
Mounia attended [[Sciences Po|Paris Institute of Political Studies]], where she earned her PhD in political science in 1993.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://applicationspub.unil.ch/interpub/noauth/php/Un/UnPers.php?PerNum=39533&menu=curri&LanCode=8|title=Unisciences - UNIL - Mounia Bennani-Chraïbi|website=applicationspub.unil.ch|access-date=2019-10-05}}</ref>

Between 2002 and 2004, Mounia was the director of Diploma of Higher Specialized Studies (DESS in [[France]]) on "Arab world, contemporary Muslim world".<ref name=":0" />

She completed her [[habilitation]] at [[Université Paris Sciences et Lettres|Université Paris Sciences et Lettres (PSL)]] in 2019.<ref name=":0" />
