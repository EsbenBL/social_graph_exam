'''Mauro Gallegati''' (born 8 March 1958) is an Italian [[New Keynesian economics|New-Keynesian]] economist, scholar of the agent based economics <ref>Farmer, J. Doyne & Foley, Duncan (August 6, 2009). "The Economy Needs Agent-Based Modelling". ''Nature''. '''460''': 685-686.</ref> and professor at [[Marche Polytechnic University]] in [[Ancona]], Italy.

== Biography ==
After having earned his PhD in economics in 1989 at [[Marche Polytechnic University]] with a thesis on financial fragility under the supervision of [[Hyman Minsky]], he has held visiting positions, both as a scholar and as a professor at [[Washington University in St. Louis]], [[University of Cambridge]], [[Stanford University]], [[Massachusetts Institute of Technology]], [[Columbia University]], [[Santa Fe Institute]], [[Brookings Institution]], [[University of Technology, Sydney]], [[Kyoto University]] and [[ETH Zurich]].<ref name="sitounivpm">[http://www.univpm.it/Entra/Engine/RAServePG.php/P/319810013893/T/Curriculun-accademico-del-docente Curriculum accademico] sul sito dell'Università Politecnica delle Marche.</ref>

He currently teaches advanced [[macroeconomics]] at Marche Polytechnic University, in the Faculty of Economics with Giorgio Fuà.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.univpm.it/Entra/Engine/RAServePG.php/P/601310011893/T/Guida-Insegnamenti|title = Guida Insegnamenti}}</ref> He has been the president of the ESHIA Society (economic science with heterogeneous interacting agents).<ref>http://eshia.bwl.uni-kiel.de/council_society.php</ref>

His research activity is mainly centred on [[complexity economics]]. Within this field of research he has published scientific works with [[Bruce Greenwald]], [[Joseph Stiglitz]] and [[Domenico Delli Gatti]].<ref>[http://ideas.repec.org/e/pga109.html Gallegati, Mauro] on IDEAS.</ref>
 
With [[Joseph Stiglitz]], Gallegati has developed a theory of [[asymmetric information]] with heterogeneous agents and its applications.<ref>Gallegati, Mauro & [[Joseph Stiglitz|Stiglitz, Joseph E.]] (1992). "Stochastic and deterministic fluctuations in a non-linear model with equity rationing". ''Giornale degli economisti ed annali di economia''. n. 51: 97–108</ref>

Gallegati is a member of the [[Institute for New Economic Thinking]].<ref>[http://ineteconomics.org/people/mauro-gallegati Mauro Gallegati] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130304210355/http://ineteconomics.org/people/mauro-gallegati |date=March 4, 2013 }}, INET website.</ref>
