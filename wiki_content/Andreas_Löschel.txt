'''Andreas Löschel''' is a German economist currently holding the chair of Energy and Resource Economics at the [[University of Münster]]. He is the director of the Centre of Applied Economic Research Münster (CEAW).<ref name="auto">{{cite web|url=https://www.wiwi.uni-muenster.de/ceres/de/team/andreas-loeschel|title=Professor Dr. Andreas Löschel - Lehrstuhl für Mikroökonomik, insb. Energie- und Ressourcenökonomik|website=Wiwi.uni-muenster.de|accessdate=13 October 2017}}</ref> His research interests include applied [[microeconomics]], [[energy economics]] and the [[economics of climate change]].<ref name="fona.de">{{cite web|url=https://www.fona.de/de/prof-andreas-loeschel-16369.html|title=Forschung für Nachhaltige Entwicklung (FONA) - FONA|website=Forschung für Nachhaltige Entwicklung (FONA)|accessdate=13 October 2017}}</ref> He is ranked among the most influential economists in his field.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sueddeutsche.de/wirtschaft/oekonomen-serie-der-gerechte-1.2701615|title=Der Gerechte|first=Markus Balser|last=Berlin|date=13 October 2017|accessdate=13 October 2017|website=Sueddeutsche.de}}</ref> In an annual ''[[Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung]]'' Ranking, he is among the 25 most influential economists in Germany (2013, 2014, 2017).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.faz.net/1.3136171|title=Ökonomenranking 2014: Die einflussreichsten Ökonomen in der Politik|first=Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung|last=GmbH|date=5 September 2014|website=FAZ.NET|accessdate=13 October 2017}}</ref>

==Education==
Löschel studied Economics at the [[University of Erlangen–Nuremberg]] and [[Wayne State University]]. In 2003, he obtained a doctorate from the [[University of Mannheim]]. He subsequently completed his habilitation in 2009 at the [[University of Oldenburg]].<ref name="fona.de"/>

==Career==
Löschel is an advisor to Aurora Energy Research Limited. Since 2005, Löschel has been a Senior Researcher at the [[Zentrum für Europäische Wirtschaftsforschung|Centre for European Economic Research]], where he has also held the position of the Director of the Environmental and Resource Economics Department between 2007 and 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.zew.de/en/presse/pressearchiv/dr-andreas-loeschel-neuer-forschungsbereichsleiter-umwelt-und-ressourcenoekonomik-umweltmanagement-am-zew/|title=ZEW Press Release: Dr. Andreas Löschel New Head of ZEW's Research Department "Environmental and Resource Economics, Environmental Management"|website=Zew.de|accessdate=13 October 2017}}</ref> From 2010 to 2014, he was a Professor for Economics focusing on Environmental and Resource Economics at the [[University of Heidelberg]]. Since 2011, he has chaired the Energy Expert Commission on the monitoring process ''Energie der Zukunft'' (Energy of the future) project of the German federal government.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bmwi.de/Redaktion/DE/Meldung/2016/20161215-expertenkommission-uebergibt-stellungnahme-zum-monitoring-bericht-energie-der-zukunft.html|title=Expertenkommission übergibt Stellungnahme zum Monitoring-Bericht "Energie der Zukunft"|first=Bundesministerium für Wirtschaft und|last=Energie|website=Bmwi.de|accessdate=13 October 2017}}</ref> In 2017, he was furthermore elected as a member of the [[National Academy of Sciences|German National Academy of Science and Technology]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.acatech.de/de/aktuelles-presse/presseinformationen-news/news-detail/artikel/acatech-mitglieder-haben-35-wissenschaftlerinnen-und-wissenschaftler-in-ihre-reihen-gewaehlt.html|title=News Detail|website=Acatech.de|accessdate=13 October 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170216050052/http://www.acatech.de/de/aktuelles-presse/presseinformationen-news/news-detail/artikel/acatech-mitglieder-haben-35-wissenschaftlerinnen-und-wissenschaftler-in-ihre-reihen-gewaehlt.html|archive-date=2017-02-16|url-status=dead}}</ref> Löschel is also a member of the Board of Trustees of the research projects "Energiesysteme der Zukunft" (Energy systems of the future) of acatech and part of the ''Research Platform Energiewende'' of the [[Federal Ministry of Education and Research (Germany)|Federal Ministry of Education and Research]].<ref name="auto"/>
Löschel is a lead author of the Working Group III contribution to the [[IPCC Fifth Assessment Report]] (2010-2014).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ipcc.ch/pdf/assessment-report/ar5/wg3/ipcc_wg3_ar5_annex-iv.pdf|title=Contributors to the IPCC WGIII Fifth Assessment Report|website=Ipcc.ch|accessdate=13 October 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171010053053/http://www.ipcc.ch/pdf/assessment-report/ar5/wg3/ipcc_wg3_ar5_annex-iv.pdf|archive-date=10 October 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.acatech.de/fileadmin/user_upload/Baumstruktur_nach_Website/Acatech/root/de/Publikationen/Stellungnahmen/ESYS_Position_Paper_Integration.pdf|title=Incorporating the German Energiewende into a comprehensive European approach|website=Acatech.de|accessdate=13 October 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171013172609/http://www.acatech.de/fileadmin/user_upload/Baumstruktur_nach_Website/Acatech/root/de/Publikationen/Stellungnahmen/ESYS_Position_Paper_Integration.pdf|archive-date=13 October 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Research==
Löschel was a visiting scholar at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) (2003), Stanford University (2005), ANU (2010, 2011), Tsinghua University Beijing (2013) and the University of Oxford (2017). Since 2014, he has been a visiting professor at the University of International Business and Economics Beijing.

His research interests include applied microeconomics, energy economics and the economics of climate change. Löschel's publications have been extensively cited.<ref>https://scholar.google.de/citations?hl=de&user=zmCHLkoAAAAJ&view_op=list_works</ref>

==Other activities==
In 2007, Löschel was a member of the [[European Commission]]’s delegation to the [[2007 United Nations Climate Change Conference|United Nations Climate Change Conference]] in [[Bali]]. He also participated in the subsequent conferences in [[2008 United Nations Climate Change Conference|Posznan]] (2008), [[2009 United Nations Climate Change Conference|Copenhagen]] (2009), [[2011 United Nations Climate Change Conference|Durban]] (2011), [[2013 United Nations Climate Change Conference|Warsaw]] (2013) and [[2014 United Nations Climate Change Conference|Lima]] (2014).

Since 2017, Löschel has been serving on the Scientific Commission of the State of Lower Saxony, under Minister-President [[Stephan Weil]].
