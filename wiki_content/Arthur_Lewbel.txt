'''Arthur Lewbel''' (born c. 1956) is the inaugural Patrick Roche Professor of [[Economics]] at [[Boston College]],<ref>[http://fmwww.bc.edu/ec/lewbel.php Lewbel’s faculty page at Boston College] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090205192656/http://fmwww.bc.edu/ec/lewbel.php |date=February 5, 2009 }}</ref> and is known in the fields of applied [[microeconomics]] and [[econometrics]].  He is an editor of Econometric Theory, former co-editor of the ''[[Journal of Business and Economic Statistics]]'',<ref>[http://pubs.amstat.org/page/jbes/editorial-board editorial board of the Journal of Business and Economic Statistics]{{dead link|date=October 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> a fellow of the [[Econometric Society]],<ref>[http://www.econometricsociety.org/fellows.asp Econometric Society Fellows] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081210090215/http://www.econometricsociety.org/fellows.asp |date=December 10, 2008 }}</ref> a fellow of the ''[[Journal of Econometrics]]'', holds a Multa Scripsit award, and is ranked number 30 on Coupe's list of top economists in the world by publication.<ref>[https://ideas.repec.org/coupe.html Coupe rankings of economists].</ref> Lewbel's economic research is mainly in the areas of micro econometrics and in [[supply and demand|consumer demand analysis]].

Lewbel holds a B.Sc. in mathematics from [[Massachusetts Institute of Technology]] (1978), and a doctorate in management (1984) from the same institution.<ref>http://econcamp.econ.sinica.edu.tw/lewbel.pdf</ref>

Lewbel is also known for his [[juggling]] contributions<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www2.bc.edu/~lewbel/acadjug.html |title=Lewbel's juggling publications and accomplishments |access-date=2009-03-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081026194657/http://www2.bc.edu/~lewbel/acadjug.html |archive-date=2008-10-26 |url-status=dead }}</ref> including "The Science of Juggling"<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www2.bc.edu/~lewbel/jugweb/science-1.html |title=The Science of Juggling |access-date=2009-03-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081221211845/http://www2.bc.edu/~lewbel/jugweb/science-1.html |archive-date=2008-12-21 |url-status=dead }}</ref> published in ''[[Scientific American]]'',<ref>''Scientific American'' November 1995, pp. 92-97</ref> and director and judge for the [[International Jugglers' Association]]'s national juggling competitions.<ref>[http://www.juggling.org/orgs/ija/rules-stage.html former IJA competition rules]</ref>
