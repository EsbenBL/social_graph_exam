'''María Julieta Kirkwood Bañados''' (5 April 1936 – 8 April 1985) was a Chilean [[Sociology|sociologist]] , political scientist, university professor and  feminist [[Activism|activist]].<ref name="ref_11"/> She is considered one of the founders and impellers of the Chilean [[Feminism|feminist]] movement in the 1980s.<ref>{{Cite book|title = Julieta Kirkwood: teórica y activista del feminismo Chileno|publisher = Editorial USACH|date = 2009|location = Santiago de Chile|isbn = 9789563030877|language = Spanish|first = Raquel|last = Olea|oclc = 664572864}}</ref> She is considered the forerunner of [[Gender studies]] in Chile.<ref name="ref_1"/><ref name="ref_4"/><ref name="ref_5"/><ref name="ref_6"/>

== Life and work ==
Daughter of Johnny Kirkwood and Julieta Bañados, she was a professor and researcher at the [[Latin American Social Sciences Institute|Latin American Faculty of Social Sciences]] (FLACSO). Her work constitute  theoretical and historical reference works in the field of the study of the feminist movement in Chile and in Latin America:<ref name="ref_4"/><ref name="ref_2"/><ref name="ref_3"/><ref name="ref_7"/> ''Ser política en Chile: las feministas y los partidos'',<ref>{{Cite book|title = Ser política en Chile: las feministas y los partidos|publisher = Facultad Latinoamericana de Ciencias Sociales|date = 1986|location = Santiago, Chile|isbn = 8489330077|language = Spanish|first = Julieta|last = Kirkwood|oclc = 14054327}}</ref> ''Tejiendo rebeldías''<ref>{{Cite book|title = Tejiendo rebeldías: escritos feministas|publisher = Centro de Estudios de la Mujer : Casa de la Mujer, La Morada|date = 1987|location = Santiago, Chile|language = Spanish|first1 = Julieta|last1 = Kirkwood|first2 = Patricia|last2 = Crispi|oclc = 22324482}}</ref> and ''Feminarios.''<ref>{{Cite book|title = Feminarios|publisher = Ediciones Documentas|date = 1987|location = Santiago, Chile|language = Spanish|first1 = Julieta|last1 = Kirkwood|first2 = Sonia|last2 = Montecino Aguirre|oclc = 18302705}}</ref> She collaborated to the magazine ''Furia'' and to the ''Boletín del Círculo de Estudios de la Mujer''.<ref name="ref_1"/>

Her theoretical work studied gender issues from a feminist point of view. She criticized the conservative feminist discourse while incorporating the [[Latin America]]n culture from an [[interculturality]] perspective.<ref name="ref_8"/><ref name="ref_9"/>

As an activist, she participated in several organisations defending the [[Women's rights|Woman's rights]], among which the ''Círculo de Estudios de la Mujer ''and the [[MEMCh 83]]. She was also one of the founders of the Feminist Movement opposing the [[Military dictatorship of Chile (1973–90)|dictatorship of Augusto Pinochet]],<ref name="ref_1"/> which motto she help promoting with [[Margarita Pisano]] was ''"Democracy in the country, in the house and in the bed"''.<ref name="ref_10"/><ref name="ref_12"/>

== Publications ==
* ''Chile: la mujer en la formulación política'' (Santiago: [[Latin American Social Sciences Institute|FLACSO]], 1981).
* ''Feminarios'' (Santiago: Documentas, 1987).
* ''El feminismo como negación del autoritarismo'' (Santiago: FLACSO, 1983).
* ''Feminismo y participación política en Chile'' (Santiago: FLACSO, 1982).
* ''Feministas y políticas'' (Santiago: FLACSO, 1984).
* ''Los nudos de la sabiduría feminista'' (Santiago: FLACSO, 1984).
* ''La política del feminismo en Chile'' (Santiago: FLACSO, 1983).
* ''Ser política en Chile : las feministas y los partidos'' (Santiago: [[LOM Ediciones]], 1982, 1986 y 2010).
* ''Ser política en Chile : los nudos de la sabiduría feminista'' (Santiago: Cuarto Propio, 1990).
* ''Tejiendo rebeldías : escritos feministas de Julieta Kirkwood'' (Santiago: CEM, 1987).
