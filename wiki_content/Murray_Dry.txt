'''Murray Dry''' is an American [[political scientist]] specializing in American [[constitutional law]], American political thought, [[political philosophy]], [[freedom of speech]], [[freedom of religion]], [[federalism]], [[separation of powers]], and the American founding.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.middlebury.edu/academics/ps/faculty/mdry |title=Faculty Profile |work=[[Middlebury College]] |access-date=2011-01-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120319202125/http://www.middlebury.edu/academics/ps/faculty/mdry |archive-date=2012-03-19 |url-status=dead }}</ref> 

Dry helped compile ''[[The Complete Anti-Federalist]]'' with his former teacher [[Herbert Storing]]. He is currently the Charles A. Dana [[Professor]] of Political Science at [[Middlebury College]], having earned his [[Bachelor of Arts|BA]], [[Master of Arts|MA]], and [[Ph.D]] at the [[University of Chicago]], where he studied under Storing and [[Leo Strauss]], among others. For the 2009â€“2010 academic year, he was a Visiting Professor at Yeshiva University. His current area of research is in the [[constitutionality]] of [[same-sex marriage]], and he recently published a book on that subject.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.middleburycampus.com/2010/10/28/staff-spotlight-murray-dry/|title=Staff Spotlight: Murray Dry|work=The Middlebury Campus}}</ref>

Murray Dry has taught many leading scholars of American government, political philosophy, and law, among them [[Suzanna Sherry]] (Vanderbilt University Law School), [[Dan Kahan]] (Yale Law School), [[James Reist Stoner Jr.|James Stoner]] (Louisiana State University), Peter Minowitz (Santa Clara University), [[Paul O. Carrese]] (United States Air Force Academy), Ayse Zarakol (Washington and Lee University), [[James Morone]] (Brown University), [[Barry Sullivan (lawyer)|Barry Sullivan]] (Loyola University), Giorgi Areshidze (Claremont McKenna College), Nicholas Sambanis (University of Pennsylvania), Odysseus Makridis (Fairleigh Dickinson University), and Victor Abundis (Academic of Classics) (Park Hill High School)<ref>{{cite web|url=http://dryconference.org/?page_id=6|title=Abstracts|work=Political Philosophy and the Constitution: A Conference in Honor of Professor Murray Dry|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110726004729/http://dryconference.org/?page_id=6|archivedate=2011-07-26}}</ref>

Also among his former students are former [[Governor of Vermont]] [[Jim Douglas]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.addisonindependent.com/201011douglas-teach-middlebury-college|title=Douglas to teach at Middlebury College|work=[[Addison County Independent]]|access-date=2011-01-31|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101115092208/http://addisonindependent.com/201011douglas-teach-middlebury-college|archive-date=2010-11-15|url-status=dead}}</ref> [[United States House of Representatives|Congressman]] [[Frank Pallone]], Congresswoman [[Barbara Comstock]], and former [[White House Press Secretary]] [[Ari Fleischer]].
