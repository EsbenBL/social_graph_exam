'''Daniel David''' (born 23 November 1972) is a [[Romania]]n academic. He is "[[Aaron T. Beck]]" professor of Clinical Psychology and Psychotherapy at the [[Babeș-Bolyai University]], [[Cluj-Napoca]]. He was the head of the Department of Clinical Psychology and Psychotherapy<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://clinicalpsychology.psiedu.ubbcluj.ro/en/staff/|title=Faculty/Staff|date=2007-07-15|newspaper=Department of Clinical Psychology and Psychotherapy|language=en-US|access-date=2016-10-14}}</ref> of the Babeş-Bolyai University between 2007 and 2012. Daniel David is also an adjunct professor at [[Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mountsinai.org/profiles/daniel-ovidiu-david|title=Daniel-Ovidiu David {{!}} Mount Sinai - New York|website=Mount Sinai Health System|access-date=2016-10-14}}</ref> and is the head of the Research Program at [http://albertellis.org/ Albert Ellis Institute]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://albertellis.org/daniel-david-phd/|title=Daniel David, Ph.D. {{!}}|website=albertellis.org|access-date=2016-10-14}}</ref> in New York. 

==Education==
David was born in [[Satu Mare]]. In 1999 he obtained a Ph.D. degree in psychology from [[Babeș-Bolyai University]] with thesis ''Mecanisme inconștiente de reactualizare a informațiilor,'' under the direction of Ioan Radu.

==National achievements==
David created the first school of [[cognitive-behavioral therapy]] (CBT) in Romania, based on international principles, recognized as such by the founders of this field such as [[Albert Ellis (psychologist)|Albert Ellis]] and [[Aaron T. Beck]]; he and his trainee also extended the application of CBT in education (e.g., rational-emotive & cognitive-behavioral education) and organizational fields (e.g., cognitive-behavioral coaching). He is a Fellow in the [http://www.academyofct.org/ Academy of Cognitive Therapy],<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://members.academyofct.org/i4a/member_directory/feResultsDetail.cfm?directory_id=5&detail_lookup_id=504|title=Academy of Cognitive Therapy|website=members.academyofct.org|access-date=2016-10-14|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161018195715/http://members.academyofct.org/i4a/member_directory/feResultsDetail.cfm?directory_id=5&detail_lookup_id=504|archive-date=18 October 2016|url-status=dead}}</ref> [[United States|U.S.A.]] and  the national representative in the Social Sciences Standing Committee at the [[European Science Foundation]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.esf.org/hosting-experts/scientific-review-groups/social-sciences-soc/scientific-review-group.html|title=Scientific Review Group : European Science Foundation|website=www.esf.org|access-date=2016-10-14}}</ref> He is among those who introduced in Romanian academic psychology the [[evolutionary psychology]] and [[genetic counseling]] as modern interdisciplinary approaches between [[psychology]] and [[biology]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.polirom.ro/catalog/carte/fundamente-de-psihologie-evolutionista-si-consiliere-genetica-integrari-ale-psihologiei-si-biologie-2548/|title=editura polirom / catalog / carte - He has promoted the reform of Romanian clinical psychology and psychotherapy based on modern principles.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://revistacultura.ro/cultura.php?articol=106|title=Cultura|last=Popescu|first=Victor|date=2006-08-17|website=revistacultura.ro|publisher=Fundația Culturală Română|access-date=2016-10-14}}</ref> Such a reform was necessary, taking into account that during the communist period of Romania both clinical psychology and psychotherapy were practically forbidden by the communist regime, and thus, the field was frozen in time. For example, after the communist era, [[Szondi]] and [[Lüscher color test|Lusher]], among other projective test, were still commonly used for clinical testing.<ref>{{Cite book|title=Tehnici Proiective|last=Minulescu|first=Mihaela|publisher=Editura Titu Maiorescu|year=2001|isbn=978-973-98628-2-0|location=Bucuresti}}</ref> David, who studied in the [[United States|U.S.A.]] for both his doctoral and postdoctoral studies,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://clinicalpsychology.psiedu.ubbcluj.ro/en/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Daniel-David-Brief-CV-October-2014.pdf|title=Academic CV|last=David|first=Daniel|date=2014-10-25|website=Department of Clinical Psychology and Psychotherapy, Babeș-Bolyai University|access-date=2016-10-14}}</ref> was one of the leaders of the first generations of psychologists after the anti-communist revolution of 1989, as since the 2000s, he has constantly been the most cited Romanian psychologist in the international literature.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://old.ad-astra.ro/cartea-alba/authors.php?topic_id=EQ|title=Ad Astra - Cartea albă a cercetării din România|website=old.ad-astra.ro|access-date=2016-10-14}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last1=Dobrean|first1=Anca|last2=Matu|first2=Silviu|year=2015|title=INTERNATIONALLY ACTIVE, VISIBLE, HIGH IMPACT, AND EMINENT ROMANIAN PSYCHOLOGISTS. ==International achievements==
He has contributed to the assimilation of cognitive science principles in the clinical field.<ref>{{Cite journal|last1=David|first1=Daniel|last2=Szentagotai|first2=Aurora|date=2006-05-01|title=Cognitions in cognitive-behavioral psychotherapies; toward an integrative model|journal=Clinical Psychology Review|volume=26|issue=3|pages=284–298|doi=10.1016/j.cpr.2005.09.003|pmid=16325974}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last1=David|first1=Daniel|last2=Miclea|first2=Mircea|last3=Opre|first3=Adrian|date=2004-04-01|title=The information-processing approach to the human mind: Basics and beyond|journal=Journal of Clinical Psychology|language=en|volume=60|issue=4|pages=353–368|doi=10.1002/jclp.10250|pmid=15022267|issn=1097-4679}}</ref> A more specific contribution was focused on developing the theory and practice of [[Rational emotive behavior therapy|rational-emotive]] and [[Cognitive behavioral therapy|cognitive-behavioral therapies]] (CBT/REBT),<ref>{{Cite book|title=Rational and irrational beliefs in human functioning and disturbances; Implication for research, theory, and practice|last1=David|first1=Daniel|last2=Lynn|first2=Steven|last3=Ellis|first3=Albert|publisher=Oxford University Press|year=2010|isbn=9780195182231|location=New York}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=Cognitive therapy of personality disorders|last1=David|first1=Daniel|last2=Freeman|first2=Arthur|publisher=Guilford Press|year=2014|isbn=9781462518050|editor-last=Beck|editor-first=Aaron|edition=Third|location=New York|chapter=Overview of cognitive behavior therapy of personality disorders|editor-last2=Davis|editor-first2=Denise|editor-last3=Freeman|editor-first3=Arthur}}</ref> which brought him both the [[Aaron T. Beck Award]] and the [[Albert Ellis Award]] of the [http://psychotherapy.psiedu.ubbcluj.ro/ International Institute for the Advanced Study of Psychotherapy and Applied Mental Health]. In 2004 he was invited as "Guest Editor" by the ''[[Journal of Clinical Psychology]]'' to organize a special issue titled: "Cognitive revolution in clinical psychology: Beyond the behavioral approach" in order to present the state-of-the-art regarding the impact of the cognitive revolution on the clinical field.<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Journal of Clinical Psychology - Volume 60, Issue 4 - Cognitive Revolution in Clinical Psychology: Beyond the Behavioral Approach |doi=10.1002/jclp.v60:4|url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/jclp.v60:4/issuetoc}}</ref> As founding editor of the ''[http://jebp.psychotherapy.ro/ Journal of Cognitive and Behavioral Psychotherapies]'' (abstracted: SSCI/Thomson ISI Web of Science; SCOPUS; PsycInfo; IBSS and full text: EBSCO; ProQuest<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://jebp.psychotherapy.ro/|title=Journal of Evidence-Based Psychotherapies|website=Journal of Evidence-Based Psychotherapies|language=en-US|access-date=2016-10-14}}</ref>), a Journal focused on evidence-based practice, he has supported the evidence-based approach in the clinical field. At this moment, is co-editor of the prestigious ''[https://www.springer.com/psychology/psychology+general/journal/10942 Journal of Rational-Emotive and Cognitive-Behavior Therapy]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.springer.com/psychology/psychology+general/journal/10942|title=Journal of Rational-Emotive & Cognitive-Behavior Therapy - incl. option to publish open access|website=springer.com|access-date=2016-10-14}}</ref> In 2013, the two research platforms, [http://clinicalpsychology.psiedu.ubbcluj.ro/skyra/ The SkyRa Platform] for Clinical Cognitive Neurosciences and the [https://web.archive.org/web/20161008055231/http://www.psytech.ro/ PsyTech-Matrix Platform] in Robotics/Robotherapy and Virtual Reality Psychotherapy, that he is coordinating as part of the [http://psychotherapy.psiedu.ubbcluj.ro/ International Institute for the Advanced Study of Psychotherapy and Applied Mental Health], have both been included in [https://web.archive.org/web/20160809074532/http://portal.meril.eu/converis-esf/publicweb/startpage?lang=1 Mapping of the European Research Infrastructure Landscape] (MERIL database).<ref name="meril_institute">{{Cite web|url=http://portal.meril.eu/converis-esf/publicweb/research_infrastructure/3446|title=MERIL - RI Information System|last=AG|first=Avedas|website=portal.meril.eu|access-date=2016-10-14|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141205102828/http://portal.meril.eu/converis-esf/publicweb/research_infrastructure/3446|archive-date=5 December 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>
