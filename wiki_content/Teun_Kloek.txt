'''Teunis (Teun) Kloek''' (born 1934) is a Dutch [[economist]] and Emeritus Professor of [[Econometrics]] at the [[Erasmus Universiteit Rotterdam]].<ref>Bas Diemel, Annelies van der Zouwen (2013), ''Erasmus School of Economics Honderd Jaar,'' Stad en Bedrijf, Rotterdam. p.284.</ref> His research interests centered on econometric methods and their applications, especially nonparametric and robust methods in econometrics.<ref>Econometric Institute (2005) ''[http://repub.eur.nl/res/pub/7860/ar2004.pdf Annual Report 2004]''. p. 81</ref>

== Biography ==
Kloek received his PhD in 1966 from the Erasmus University Rotterdam for the thesis "Indexcijfers : enige methodologische aspecten" (Index : some methodological aspects) under supervision of [[Henri Theil]].

Kloek was appointed Professor of Econometrics at the [[Erasmus Universiteit Rotterdam]] in 1967. With [[Alexander Rinnooy Kan]] and later [[Harm Bart]], he was co-director of the [[Econometric Institute]] from 1982 to 1992 as successor of [[Willem Somermeyer]] and was succeeded by [[Ton Vorst]].<ref name = "PHF 2005">[[Philip Hans Franses]] (2005) ''[http://repub.eur.nl/res/pub/7860/ar2004.pdf Annual Report 2004: Econometric Institute]'' p. 7</ref> Since his retirement in 1997<ref>Kritisch & constructief: 40 jaar grensverkenningen in de econometrie: liber amicorum voor prof. dr. T. Kloek. Econometrisch Instituut, Erasmus Universiteit, 1997.</ref> Kloek has been affiliated with the [[Tinbergen Institute]].

Some of Kloek's most notable doctoral students were [[Herman K. van Dijk]] (1984) and [[Philip Hans Franses]] (1991).<ref>[http://www.eur.nl/ese/english/departments/department_of_econometrics/research/publications/theses/1990_1999/ Theses EUR department of econometrics 1990-1999]</ref>

Kloek was elected fellow of the [[Econometric Society]] in 1978<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=https://www.econometricsociety.org/society/organization-and-governance/fellows |title=Econometric Society Fellows |language= |publisher=Econometric Society |date=December 2015 |access-date=21 February 2016}}</ref> and of the ''[[Journal of Econometrics]]'', and honorary fellow of the [[Tinbergen Institute]].
