'''Sheri E. Berman''' is a Professor of Political Science at [[Barnard College]], [[Columbia University]].<ref>{{Citation |url=https://polisci.barnard.edu/profiles/sheri-berman|title=Barnard College- Sheri Berman}}</ref>  She is the author of scholarly books and articles on European social democracy, fascism, populism and the development of democracies and dictatorships.

She has also published in a wide variety of non-scholarly publications, including the  ''[[New York Times]]'',  ''[[Washington Post]]'',  ''[[The Wall Street Journal]]'',  ''[[Foreign Affairs]]'',  ''[[Foreign Policy]]'',  ''[[Vox (website)|VOX]]'',  ''[[The Guardian]]''  and ''[[Dissent (American magazine)|Dissent]]''. She is on the boards of the ''[[Journal of Democracy]]'',  ''[[Political Science Quarterly]]'',  ''Dissent'' and ''Persuasion''.

==Education==
Berman has a BA in political science from [[Yale University]] and an MA and PhD in government from [[Harvard University]].<ref name="cv">{{cite web|last1=Berman|first1=Sheri|date=December 2020|title=CV|url=https://sites.google.com/view/sheriberman/cv?authuser=0|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=3 February 2020|website=|publisher=Barnard College}}</ref>

==Honors==
In 2008 she was awarded an honorary doctorate by [[Uppsala University]], Sweden<ref name=cv /><ref name="Waara">{{cite web |last1=Waara |first1=Anneli |title=Samhällsvetarna utser tre nya hedersdoktorer |url=https://www.uu.se/nyheter-press/nyheter/artikel/?id=3514&na=1 |website=Uppsala universitet|date=10 October 2007 |access-date=24 August 2021}}</ref>

She served as Chair of the Barnard political science department from 2009 to 2012 and Chair of the Council on European Studies from 2014 o 2017.<ref>[https://barnard.edu/sites/default/files/2019-08/Sheri%20Berman%20CV.pdf CV Sheri Berman]</ref>
