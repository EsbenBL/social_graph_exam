'''Jacqueline Peschard Mariscal''' is a Mexican sociologist who specializes in electoral integrity and democracy studies. She was President Commissioner of the [[:es:Instituto Nacional de Transparencia, Acceso a la Información y Protección de Datos Personales|Federal Institute for Access to Public Information]] from 2009 until 2013. She also served as Counselor to the [[Instituto Nacional Electoral]] from 1997 to 2003.<ref name="Jacqueline Peschard, PhD">{{cite web |title=Jacqueline Peschard, PhD |url=http://www.cba.org/CBA/cle/PDF/PRV11_Bio_Peschard_E.pdf |website=International Conference of Information Commissioners |publisher=Canadian Bar Association |accessdate=30 March 2020}}</ref> In 2010, she was elected president of the Iberoamerican Network of Data Protection (RIPD), which is a forum for promoting the universal right to data protection across Latin America. 

She currently teaches at the [[Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México]] in [[Mexico City]].<ref name="Jacqueline Peschard, PhD"/> 

==Education==
Peschard has bachelors and masters degrees from [[UNAM]] and a doctorate from [[:es:El Colegio de Michoacán|El Colegio de Michoacán]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Jacqueline Peschard |url=http://inicio.ifai.org.mx/PdfAudio/CV_Jacqueline_Peschard_Mariscal.pdf |publisher=Instituto Nacional de Transparencia, Acceso a la Información y Protección de Datos Personales |accessdate=30 March 2020}}</ref>

==Research==
She has taught at [[El Colegio de México]], the [[Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México]] and the [[Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México]]. 

She has been a member of the [[Sistema Nacional de Investigadores]] '''Level II''' since October 2004. 

In 2004, she was an advisor to the [[United Nations]] Electoral Assistance Division on election stability in [[Iraq]].<ref name="‘Democratic Consolidation’ in Mexico">{{cite web |last1=Senderling |first1=Jon |title='Democratic Consolidation' in Mexico |url=https://www.utdallas.edu/news/archive/2005/peschard-lecture.html |website=University of Texas at Dallas |publisher=UT Dallas |accessdate=30 March 2020}}</ref>
