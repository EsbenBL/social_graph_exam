'''Olivier Bobineau''' (born 6 March 1972, [[La Roche-sur-Yon]]) is a French [[Sociology|sociologist]] specialized in the sociology of religion.

He teaches sociology at the [[Institut d'Etudes Politiques de Paris]], the [[Institut Catholique de Paris]] and the [[École supérieure des sciences économiques et commerciales]], he is also Director of the [[:fr:Institut du sens politique|Institut du sens politique]]. In January 2006, he became scientific collaborator of the officer of the Central Bureau of Religious Affairs, Ministry of Interior.

When he was a teenager, he discovered the [[Bible]] and sociology through a [[priest]], and attended lectures in IEP-Bordeaux. For a while he was active in the [[Socialist Party (France)|Socialist Party]]. Then he became [[agrégé]] of economic and social sciences and earned a PhD in sociology of religion at IEP-Paris.<ref>{{cite web |title=Olivier Bobineau, intellectuel des marges |url=http://www.scienceshumaines.com/olivier-bobineau-2c-intellectuel-des-marges_fr_25842.html |publisher=Sciences Humaines |first=Laurent |last=Testot |language=French |accessdate=11 August 2010}}</ref>

Bobineau criticized the work of the [[MIVILUDES]] on cults.<ref>{{cite web |title=Lutte contre les sectes: " La Miviludes, police des esprits " |url=http://www.rue89.com/2009/06/08/lutte-contre-les-sectes-la-miviludes-police-des-esprits |publisher=Rue 89 |first=Dominique |last=Albertini |date=8 June 2009 |language=French |accessdate=11 August 2010}}</ref>  His book ''Le satanisme. Quel Danger pour la société ?'' raised some controversy since it contradicted the observations made by the MIVILUDES (for which he was scientific advisor in 2005), claiming that [[Satanist]]s are just a few hundred in France and they pose no danger.<ref>{{cite web |title= Le satanisme ne présente aucun danger pour la société  |url=http://www.20minutes.fr/article/220409/France-Le-satanisme-ne-presente-aucun-danger-pour-la-societe.php |publisher=20 Minutes |first=Catherine |last=Fournier |date=19 March 2009 |language=French |accessdate=11 August 2010}}</ref> He also made public statements about the [[burqa]] and [[Islam]].<ref>{{cite web |title=L'arabo-islamophobie, ou la " peur des barbus ": un mal très français |url=http://www.saphirnews.com/L-arabo-islamophobie-ou-la-peur-des-barbus-un-mal-tres-francais_a11386.html |publisher=Saphir News |first=Olivier |last=Bobineau |language=French |accessdate=11 August 2010}}</ref>
