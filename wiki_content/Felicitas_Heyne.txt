{{short description|German psychologist and book author}}

'''Felicitas Heyne''' is a [[German language|German]] psychologist and book author. She is also the developer of the iPersonic system for personal development, which is available in 26 languages.<ref name="iPersonic">{{cite web |url=http://www.ipersonic.com |title=iPersonic |accessdate=2015-06-16 }}</ref>

== Life ==

She was born in 1966 in [[Heidelberg]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Author profile at Random House/Goldmann Publishing|url=http://www.randomhouse.de/Autor/Felicitas_Heyne/p424307.rhd|accessdate=3 January 2013|language=de}}</ref>   She worked at first for some years in economics before she studied psychology at the University of Koblenz-Landau.<ref>{{cite web|title=List of diploma thesis of the University of Koblenz-Landau (Germany)|url=http://www.uni-koblenz-landau.de/bibliothek/dateien/pdf/diplomarbeitenpsychostandapril2011.pdf|access-date=3 January 2013|language=de|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131229050640/http://www.uni-koblenz-landau.de/bibliothek/dateien/pdf/diplomarbeitenpsychostandapril2011.pdf|archive-date=29 December 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref>  She lives and works in [[Annweiler am Trifels]].<ref name=bdp>{{cite web|title=Profile at the Association of German Professional Psychologists  (Berufsverband Deutscher Psychologinnen und Psychologen)|url=http://heyne.imbdp.de/|accessdate=3 January 2013|language=de}}</ref>
