'''Dr John Francis Leader''', also known as JFL, is an Irish [[Psychology|psychologist]] and [[Cognitive science|cognitive scientist]] who researches at [[University College Dublin]], specialises in personal leadership, experiential learning and [[Mixed Reality|mixed reality]] therapy, and is the host of #bodymindself.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lo4DPYYQrGc|title=JFL TEDx Talk: 'Therapy of Tomorrow'|publisher=[[TED Talks]]|accessdate=20 August 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://jfl.com|title=JFL.com Website|publisher=JFL|accessdate=20 August 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/user/JFLdotCOM|title=#bodymindself YouTube Channel|publisher=JFL|accessdate=20 August 2019}}</ref>

Combining multimedia techniques with current psychological research, Leader's work aims to make therapy more fun, immersive and effective—a type of theme park meets therapy experience.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://jfl.com/x/the-therapy-of-tomorrow-initiative-lets-crowdsource-therapy-using-multimedia/|title=Change.org Therapy of Tomorrow campaign|publisher=[[Change.org]]|accessdate=20 August 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://jfl.com/about/john-francis-leader/|title=JFL Bio|publisher=JFL|accessdate=20 August 2019}}</ref>
