'''Marianne Vendel Thyrring''' is a Danish policy maker and [[political scientist]]. She has been Director of the [[Danish Meteorological Institute]] and Department Head at the [[Ministry of Environment (Denmark)|Ministry of Environment of Denmark]].

==Life and career==
Thyrring graduated from [[Aarhus University]] in 1982 with a [[Cand.scient.pol.]] degree.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.altinget.dk/person/marianne-thyrring |title=Marianne Thyrring |publisher=Altinget |date=December 17, 2019 |access-date=6 November 2020}}</ref> From 1984 to 1993, she worked in the [[Ministry of Taxation (Denmark)|Danish Ministry of Taxation]].<ref name=profile>{{cite web |url=https://ledelseskom.dk/personer/marianne-thyrring |language=da |title=Marianne Thyrring |publisher=Ledelseskommissionen |access-date=6 November 2020}}</ref> She then worked for a year as an attaché at the Representation of Denmark to the European Union.<ref name=profile/>

In 1995, Thyrring became a Finance Manager at the Ministry of the Environment.<ref name=profile/> From 1999 to 2003, she was Deputy Chief of Staff in the Cabinet of Environment Commissioner [[Ritt Bjerregaard]].<ref name=profile/> In 2003 she became head of the environmental policy area at the Danish Ministry of the Environment.<ref name=profile/> Then from 2007 until 2012, she was Head of Department in the Ministry of the Environment.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.altinget.dk/artikel/studsgaard-ny-departementschef-i-miljoeministeriet  |language=da |title=Studsgaard ny departementschef i Miljøministeriet |work=Altinget |first=Christian Juhl |last=Mølgaard |date=January 31, 2013 |access-date=6 November 2020}}</ref>

In 2012, she left the Ministry of the Environment when she was appointed by the [[Ministry of the Interior (Denmark)|Ministry of the Interior of Denmark]] to conduct a study of the state of municipal-level democracy in Denmark.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.altinget.dk/artikel/thyrring-soegte-ikke-selv-vaek |language=da |title=Thyrring søgte ikke selv væk |work=Altinget |first=Anders |last=Jerking |date=December 18, 2012 |access-date=6 November 2020}}</ref> In 2013, she became Director of the Danish Meteorological Institute.<ref name=profile/> At the Danish Meteorological Institute, she was involved in making the organization more public-facing, and making use of the large stores of weather-related data that it had.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://public.wmo.int/en/resources/bulletin/data-driven-%E2%80%93-way-through-storm |title=Data-Driven – A Way Through the Storm |publisher=World Meteorological Organization |year=2019 |access-date=6 November 2020}}</ref>

Thyrring was awarded the Knight's Cross of the 1st degree of the [[Order of the Dannebrog]] in 2012.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://sermitsiaq.ag/kl/node/116187 |language=da |title=Asii og Hermann er riddere af Dannebrog |work=[[Sermitsiaq (newspaper)|Sermitsiaq]] |first=Søren Duran |last=Duus |date=January 17, 2012 |access-date=6 November 2020}}</ref>
