{{short description|French sociologist}}
'''Marie Jaisson''' is a [[sociology|sociologist]] studying the sociology of medical practices and of biological phenomena, and the history of Sociology. After a Ph.D. in Sociology at EHESS (Paris), she was junior professor at the [University of Tours] and she is full professor at the [[University Sorbonne Paris-Nord]] (France).
