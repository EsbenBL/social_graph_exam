'''Frank Baumgartner''' is an American political scientist, currently the [[Richard J. Richardson]] Distinguished Professor at the [[University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill]] and formerly the Distinguished Professor and Bruce R. Miller and Dean D. LaVigne Professor at [[Penn State University]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.unc.edu/~fbaum/ |title=Frank Baumgartner |publisher=unc.edu |accessdate=April 9, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=zBQK-VkAAAAJ&hl=en |title=Frank Baumgartner |publisher=scholar.google.com |accessdate=April 9, 2017}}</ref>  He received all of his academic degrees at the [[University of Michigan]] (BA 1980, MA 1983, PhD 1986).
