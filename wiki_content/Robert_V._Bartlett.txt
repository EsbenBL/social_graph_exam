'''Robert V. Bartlett''' is an American political scientist, currently the Gund Professor of the Liberal Arts at [[University of Vermont]], previously the Fulbright Distinguished Chair at the [[Polytechnic University of Turin]], the [[Frank Church]] Distinguished Professor at [[Boise State University]], Senior [[Fulbright Program|Fulbright Scholar]] at [[Trinity College, Dublin|Trinity College]], [[University of Dublin]], and a Senior Fulbright Scholar at the Centre For Resource Management at [[Lincoln University (New Zealand)|Lincoln University]] and [[University of Canterbury]],<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |url=https://www.uvm.edu/~polisci/?Page=RobertBartlett.php |title=Robert V. Bartlett |publisher=uvm.edu |accessdate=December 18, 2016}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web |url=https://www.worldcat.org/identities/lccn-n80005889/ |title=Bartlett, Robert V. |publisher=worldcat.org |accessdate=December 18, 2016}}</ref> He was educated at [[Indiana University Bloomington]].<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />
