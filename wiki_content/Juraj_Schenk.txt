'''Juraj Schenk''' (born May 6, 1948) is former [[Foreign Minister of Slovakia]] from 1994 to 1996 in cabinet of [[Vladimír Mečiar]].

Schenk studied sociology at the [[Comenius University|Univezita Komenského]], [[Bratislava]] and is working there since 1972. In 1994 he became a professor of sociology and is teaching there until now.

His main professional interests include sociological methodology, [[Self-organization|self-organisation]] of social systems ([[Synergetics (Haken)|synergetics]], [[chaos theory]]), causal modelling, construction of [[Sociological theory|sociological theories]] and [[empirical research]].

His current research activities concern with [[Alexander Hirner]]'s methodological conception, scaling in sociological research, chaos theory and empirical research.
