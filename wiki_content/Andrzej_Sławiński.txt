'''Andrzej Sławiński''' (born July 31, 1951 in [[Warsaw]]) is a [[Poles|Polish]] [[economist]]<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/2010-12-14/slawinski-says-polish-zloty-moves-won-t-affect-rate-policy.html|title=Slawinski Says Polish Zloty Moves Won't Affect Rate Policy|last=Rozlal|first=Monika|date=14 December 2010|work=[[Bloomberg L.P.|Bloomberg]]|access-date=28 March 2011}}</ref> and Professor of Economics at the [[Warsaw School of Economics]].<ref>{{cite web| title= Collegium Invisibile | url= http://www.ci.edu.pl/index.php?id=tutorzy&kat=Czlonkowie| date= 7 May 2013 | archiveurl  = http://archive.today/XHZ3b
| archivedate = 16 September 2013}}</ref> He is a member of the [[Council of Monetary Policies]] since 2004 and a fellow of [[Collegium Invisibile]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ci.edu.pl/index.php?id=tutorzy&kat=Czlonkowie |title=List of Fellows |publisher=ci.edu.pl |access-date=25 April 2011}}</ref>

He received his [[Master of Arts|M.A.]] in economics in 1973 and [[Ph.D.]] in [[international finance]] in 1979 both from the [[Warsaw School of Economics]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Polityka pieniężna  Skład Rady Polityki Pieniężnej |url=https://www.nbp.pl/home.aspx?f=/polityka_pieniezna/rada/2_kadencja/slawinski.ht |website=Narodowego Banku Polskiego |publisher=NBP |access-date=19 March 2021 |language=Polish}}</ref>

His research work concentrates on [[central bank]]s functioning, financial crises in developing economies and financial (especially [[Derivative (finance)|derivatives]]) markets. Author of numerous publications. In September 2010, he was appointed to the Economic Institute of the [[National Bank of Poland]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Wilkowicz |first1=Łukasz |title=Andrzej Sławiński szefem Instytutu Ekonomicznego NBP |url=https://www.rp.pl/artykul/542195-Andrzej-Slawinski-szefem-Instytutu-Ekonomicznego-NBP.html |access-date=19 March 2021 |agency=Rzeczpospolita |publisher=Gremi Media SA |date=29 September 2010 |language=Polish}}</ref>
