'''Alfred E. Johnson''' was an [[anthropologist]] and [[archaeologist]] at the [[University of Kansas]].<ref>Smith, Carlyle (1985) ''A History of Anthropology at the University of Kansas''</ref>

==Early life and education==
Johnson was born in [[Kansas]].

He received his B.A. in [[anthropology]] from the [[University of Kansas]] and his M.A. and Ph.D. in anthropology from the [[University of Arizona]]. His dissertation research focused on [[Pueblo Indian]] culture.

==Post-graduate career==
Johnson joined the faculty of the University of Kansas in 1965.<ref>{{cite web|title=Archaeological Collections|url=http://www2.ku.edu/~arc/collections/history.shtml|website=ku.edu|publisher=The University of Kansas|accessdate=19 September 2014}}</ref>
