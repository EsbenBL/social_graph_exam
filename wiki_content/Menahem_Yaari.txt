'''Menahem E. Yaari''' ('''מנחם יערי'''; born April 26, 1935) is an Israeli [[economist]] who has been the S.A. Schonbrunn Professor of Mathematical Economics at [[The Hebrew University of Jerusalem]], and was the President of the [[Open University of Israel]] from 1992 to 1997. <ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.academy.ac.il/SystemFiles/19733.pdf|title=Prof. Menahem E. Yaari|publisher= [[Israel Academy of Sciences and Humanities]]}}</ref> He was awarded the [[Israel Prize]] in 1987, the [[Rothschild Prize]] in the Social Sciences in 1994, and the [[EMET Prize]] in the Social Sciences in 2012.

==Biography==
Yaari was born in [[Jerusalem]] in [[Mandatory Palestine]].<ref name="auto"/>

He obtained a B.A. in Economics and Philosophy from [[The Hebrew University of Jerusalem]] in 1958, and a Ph.D. in Economics and Statistics from [[Stanford University]] in 1962.<ref name="auto"/>

From 1962 to 1967 Yaari was an Assistant Professor and Associate Professor at [[Yale University]] in New Haven, Connecticut, and a member of the [[Cowles Foundation for Research in Economics]] at the university.<ref name="auto"/><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ujmIhF6W8tIC&q=%22Menahem+Yaari%22&pg=PR11|title=The Collected Scientific Work of David Cass|first=David|last=Cass|date= 2011|publisher=[[Emerald Group Publishing]]|isbn=9780857246417|via=Google Books}}</ref>

From 1967 to 1970 he was a Senior Lecturer and Associate Professor of Economics and Philosophy of Science at [[the Hebrew University of Jerusalem]].<ref name="auto"/> Since 1971 Yaari has been the S.A. Schonbrunn Professor of Mathematical Economics at the university (emeritus since 1998), and from 1971 to 1973 he was the Chairman of the university's Department of Economics.<ref name="auto"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://news.yale.edu/2015/12/01/memoriam-herbert-scarf-pioneering-economist-and-inspiring-teacher|title=In memoriam: Herbert Scarf, pioneering economist and inspiring teacher|date=December 1, 2015|work=[[Yale News]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ynet.co.il/articles/0,7340,L-2540534,00.html|title=פרופ' יערי: "ככלכלן, אני לא מוצא היגיון בתוכנית"|newspaper=[[Ynet]]}}</ref>

From 1968 to 1975 Yaari was an editor of ''[[Econometrica]]''.<ref name="auto"/>

[[Israel Academy of Sciences and Humanities]], since 1993 he has been a foreign honorary member of the [[American Economic Association]], since 1996 he has been a Fellow of the [[Berlin-Brandenburg Academy of Sciences]].<ref name="auto1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.jpost.com/HEALTH-SCIENCE/Holocaust-survivor-gives-1-million-in-scholarships-to-doctoral-students-498568|title=Holocaust survivor Yaari was awarded the [[Israel Prize]] in 1987, the [[Rothschild Prize]] in the Social Sciences in 1994, and the [[EMET Prize]] in the Social Sciences in 2012.<ref name="auto"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.news1.co.il/Archive/006-D-1024-00.html|title=יערי מנחם|work=[[News1]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=wyckAQAAIAAJ&q=%D7%9E%D7%A0%D7%97%D7%9D+%D7%99%D7%A2%D7%A8%D7%99|title=Israel Government Year Book|first=Israel Merkaz|last=ha-hasbarah|date=1987|publisher=Central Office of Information, [[Israeli Prime Minister's Office]]|via=Google Books}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://emetprize.com/|title=פרס א.מ.ת. &#124; פרס האמנות, המדע והתרבות|website=emetprize.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://web.nli.org.il/sites/nli/english/library/globalforum/members/pages/menahem_yaari.aspx|title=Menahem E.Yaari|publisher=[[National Library of Israel]]}}</ref>
