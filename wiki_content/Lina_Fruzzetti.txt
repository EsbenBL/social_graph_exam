'''Lina Fruzzetti''' is an American [[Cultural anthropology|cultural anthropologist]] and documentary [[Filmmaking|filmmaker]]. Since 1975, she has been a professor of [[anthropology]] at [[Brown University]] in the United States.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Lina Fruzzetti {{!}} Center for Contemporary South Asia|url=https://watson.brown.edu/southasia/people/lina-fruzzetti|access-date=2021-02-02|website=watson.brown.edu}}</ref> Apart from having published ethnographic studies about rural communities and [[Gender role|gender relations]] in East Africa, India and Tanzania, she is the author of several ethnographic films.<ref>{{Cite web|date=1997-06-06|title=Anthropologist Lina Fruzzetti and Tanzania|url=http://www.brown.edu/Administration/George_Street_Journal/Fruzzetti.html|access-date=2021-02-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/19970606140555/http://www.brown.edu/Administration/George_Street_Journal/Fruzzetti.html|archive-date=1997-06-06}}</ref> These films were written and co-directed with her husband, Ákos Östör, cultural anthropologist and [[professor emeritus]] of anthropology at [[Wesleyan University]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Lina Fruzzetti {{!}} DER Filmmaker Bio|url=https://www.der.org/resources/filmmaker-bios/lina-fruzzetti/|access-date=2021-02-02|website=www.der.org|language=en-US}}</ref> Since 2016 Fruzzetti is also a [[Fellow]] at the [[Jawaharlal Nehru University]] Institute for Advanced Studies (JNIAS) in New Delhi, India.

== Ethnographic films ==
Fruzzetti's [[ethnographic film]]s include ''Seed and Earth'' (1994), ''Fishers of Dar'' (2002), ''Singing Pictures'' (2005), ''Songs of a Sorrowful Man'' (2009) and ''In My Mother's House'' (2016).<ref>{{Cite web|title=In My Mother's House|url=https://store.der.org/in-my-mothers-house-p740.aspx|access-date=2021-02-02|website=Documentary Educational Resources Online Store|language=en}}</ref> ''Singing Pictures,'' distributed by [[Documentary Educational Resources]], won an award at the XV International Festival of Ethnological Film (Belgrade November 1–5, 2006).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.etnografskimuzej.rs/e0303153.htm |work=XV International Festival of Ethnological Film |title=Awards |publisher=[[Ethnographic Museum (Belgrade)]] |date=November 2006 |access-date=2010-10-19 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111007000553/http://www.etnografskimuzej.rs/e0303153.htm |archive-date=2011-10-07 }}</ref>
