'''Shlomo Raber Eckstein''' (1 November 1929 – 12 January 2020) was an Israeli economist, and President of [[Bar-Ilan University]].<ref name="auto1">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=l3pBAQAAIAAJ&q=Shlomo+Eckstein+germany|title=Directory of Members|first=American Economic|last=Association|date= 1969|via=Google Books}}</ref><ref name="bnf"/>

==Biography==

Eckstein was born in [[Wiesbaden, Germany]].<ref name="auto1"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=t8KW_2YC6CoC&q=%22Shlomo+Eckstein%22+germany|title=Condemnation, Commemoration, Determination: Bar-Ilan Responds to a National Tragedy, November-December 1995|date= 1996|publisher=Bar-Ilan University, Spokesman's Office, English Communications|via=Google Books}}</ref><ref name="auto2">{{cite web|url=https://www.chabad.org/news/article_cdo/aid/4622209/jewish/Shlomo-Eckstein-91-Former-President-of-Bar-Ilan-University.htm|title=Shlomo Eckstein, 91, Former President of Bar-Ilan University - Economics professor served in many philanthropic positions|work=Chabad News|date=January 20, 2020}}</ref> His family emigrated from Germany before World War II to Mexico, which is where he was raised.<ref name="auto2"/>

He earned a degree in Economics at the [[National University of Mexico]] in 1957, and earned a Ph.D. in Economics from [[Harvard University]] in 1964.<ref name="auto1"/><ref name="auto2"/> His 1963 dissertation was on "Collective Farming In Mexico."<ref name="auto1"/>
==Academic career==
Later, he made [[aliyah]] to Israel, becoming an Israeli citizen, and became a professor of Economics and founded the Department of Economics at [[Bar-Ilan University]] in 1960.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.biupress.co.il/website_en/index.asp?action=author_page&aet_id=25|title=Author Page - Shlomo Eckstein |website=Bar-Ilan University Press}}</ref><ref name="auto2"/> He was Chairman of the Department from 1961 to 1965, and from 1968 to 1971.<ref name="auto">{{cite web|url=https://econ.biu.ac.il/en/eckstein|title=Eckstein Shlomo &#124; Department of Economics |website=Bar-Ilan University}}</ref> He was the Rector at the university from 1978 to 1982.<ref name="auto"/> He was the university’s President from 1992 to 1996, succeeding [[Zvi Arad]] and followed by [[Moshe Kaveh]].<ref name="auto2"/><ref name="auto"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www1.biu.ac.il/indexE.php?id=6551&pt=1&pid=988&level=5&cPath=35,983,988 |title=Bar-Ilan Presidents &#124; Bar Ilan University |publisher=.biu.ac.il |date= |accessdate=2020-02-18}}</ref>

He died on 12 January 2020 at the age of 90.<ref>[https://www1.biu.ac.il/index.php?id=2&pt=20&pid=4&level=2&cPath=4&type=2&news=4212 Bar-Ilan University death notice]</ref><ref name="bnf">{{BNF|137378307}}</ref>
