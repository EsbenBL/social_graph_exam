'''Asher Cohen'''  ({{lang-he|אשר כהן}}) is an Israeli psychologist. He has been the 14th President of the [[Hebrew University of Jerusalem]] since September 1, 2017, and holds the university's Samuel Sturman Chair in Psychology.

==Biography==
Asher Cohen graduated from the [[Hebrew University of Jerusalem]] with a B.A. in Economics and an M.A. in Psychology.<ref name=huji>{{cite web|url=https://new.huji.ac.il/en/page/454 |title=Office of the President &#124; האוניברסיטה העברית בירושלים &#124; The Hebrew University of Jerusalem |publisher=New.huji.ac.il |date=2017-09-01 |accessdate=2020-01-18}}</ref> He completed his doctoral and post-doctoral studies at the [[University of Oregon]].<ref name=huji/> 
==Academic career==
Cohen was a senior lecturer and Assistant Professor at [[Indiana University]].<ref name="google1">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-6SgBgAAQBAJ&q=%22Asher+Cohen%22+%22Rector%22&pg=PA276 |title=Cognitive Modeling in Perception and Memory: A Festschrift for Richard M. Shiffrin|author= J G W Raaijmakers|author2= Robert Goldstone|author3= Mark Steyvers|author4= Amy Criss|author5= Robert Nosofsky|publisher=Psychology Press |date=2015 |isbn=9781134629251|accessdate=2020-01-18}}</ref><ref name="autogenerated1">[https://www.afhu.org/2017/11/06/president-professor-asher-cohen/ "Spotlights; President Professor Asher Cohen,"] November 6, 2017.</ref>

Cohen returned in the early 1990s to the Hebrew University’s Department of Psychology, in the Faculty of Social Sciences.<ref name=huji/> From 2008 to 2012, he was the head of the university's Department of Psychology.<ref>{{cite web|last=Trachtman |first=Chaim |url=https://jewishweek.timesofisrael.com/the-latest-from-the-classroom-and-beyond-3/ |title=The Latest From The Classroom, And Beyond &#124; ''Jewish Week'' |work=Times of Israel|date=2017-08-23 |accessdate=2020-01-18}}</ref> From 2012 to 2017, Cohen was the Hebrew University’s Rector.<ref name="autogenerated1"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://new.huji.ac.il/en/article/35133 |title=Prof. Asher Cohen Elected President of the Hebrew University of Jerusalem &#124; האוניברסיטה העברית בירושלים &#124; The Hebrew University of Jerusalem |publisher=New.huji.ac.il |date=2017-09-01 |accessdate=2020-01-18}}</ref>

Cohen has been the 14th President of the [[Hebrew University of Jerusalem]] since September 1, 2017, succeeding [[Menahem Ben-Sasson]], who was President of the University for eight years.<ref name=huji/> He holds the university's Samuel Sturman Chair in Psychology.<ref name="autogenerated1"/>
