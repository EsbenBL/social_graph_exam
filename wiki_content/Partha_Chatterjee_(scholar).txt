'''Partha Chatterjee''' (born 5 November 1947) is an Indian political scientist and anthropologist.<ref name="mesaas">{{cite web|title=Faculty page: Partha Chatterjee|url=http://www.columbia.edu/cu/mesaas/faculty/directory/chatterjee.html|website=Middle Eastern, South Asian, and African Studies|publisher=www.columbia.edu|access-date=5 June 2017|language=en}}</ref> He was the director of the [[Centre for Studies in Social Sciences, Calcutta]] from 1997 to 2007 and continues as an honorary professor of political science.<ref name="cssscal" /> He is also a professor of anthropology and South Asian studies at [[Columbia University]] and a member of the [[Subaltern Studies Collective]].<ref name="mesaas" />

Chatterjee received the [[Fukuoka Asian Culture Prize]] in 2009<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.asianmonth.com/prize/english/winner/index.html|title=Past Laureates: Fukuoka Prize|publisher=[[Fukuoka]]|access-date=19 August 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171230230451/http://www.asianmonth.com/prize/english/winner/index.html|archive-date=30 December 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Education==
He completed a [[Bachelor of Arts|BA]] (1967) and an [[Master of Arts|M.A]] (1970) in political science from [[Presidency College, Calcutta]] and [[Calcutta University]] respectively. He completed his Ph.D. (1972) in international relations from the [[University of Rochester]].<ref name="cv" /><ref name="wheeler">{{cite web|last1=Wheeler|first1=William|title=Presidential Lectures: Partha Chatterjee|url=https://prelectur.stanford.edu/lecturers/chatterjee/|website=prelectur.stanford.edu|access-date=5 June 2017}}</ref> His father, Santosh Chatterjee, was a Professor of Political Science at Calcutta University.{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}}

==Career==
He was the professor of political science and served as a director of the [[Centre for Studies in Social Sciences, Calcutta]] and is currently a professor (honorary) of the CSSSC and professor of [[anthropology]] and South Asian studies at [[Columbia University]] in [[New York City|New York]].<ref name="cssscal">{{cite web|title=Centre for Social Sciences, Calcutta|url=http://www.cssscal.org/partha_chatterjee_contact.html|website=www.cssscal.org|access-date=5 June 2017}}</ref> He was a founder-member of the [[Subaltern Studies Collective]].<ref name="mesaas" />

He is a [[joint]]-[[editor in chief|editor]] of ''Baromash'', a biannual Bengali literary journal published from Calcutta. In addition to numerous books in English, he has published collections of essays in Bengali.{{citation needed|date=March 2016}}
