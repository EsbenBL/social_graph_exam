'''Klaas Henderikus Willem Knot''' (born 14 April 1967, in [[Bedum]], Netherlands) is a [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[economist]] and central banker, who is the current President of the Dutch [[central bank]] [[De Nederlandsche Bank]] (DNB).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nu.nl/economie/2519839/klaas-knot-wordt-nieuwe-president-dnb.html |title=Klaas Knot wordt nieuwe president van DNB |date=2011-05-20 |website=NU.nl |language=nl |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110522082649/http://www.nu.nl/economie/2519839/klaas-knot-wordt-nieuwe-president-dnb.html |archive-date=2011-05-22 |url-status=live}}</ref> In this capacity he also serves as a member of the [[Governing Council of the European Central Bank]], and of the Board of Governors of the [[International Monetary Fund#Members.27 quotas and voting power.2C and board of governors|International Monetary Fund]]. Knot also holds a position as Professor in Monetary Stability at the [[University of Amsterdam]]<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://www.foliaweb.nl/organisatie/president-de-nederlandsche-bank-benoemd-tot-hoogleraar/ | title=President De Nederlandsche Bank benoemd tot hoogleraar |website=Folia.nl |date=2015-01-05 |first=Dirk |last=Wolthekker |language=nl |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150608223103/www.foliaweb.nl/organisatie/president-de-nederlandsche-bank-benoemd-tot-hoogleraar/ |archive-date=2015-06-08 |url-status=live}}</ref> and honorary professor at the [[University of Groningen]].<ref>{{Cite web | url=https://www.rug.nl/staff/k.h.w.knot/ | title=Prof. Dr. K.H.W. (Klaas) Knot |publisher=Rijksuniversiteit Groningen |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211108193512/https://www.rug.nl/staff/k.h.w.knot/ |archive-date=2021-11-08 |url-status=live}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Knot was born in the town of [[Bedum]] near [[Groningen]]. After completing his secondary education ([[Voorbereidend wetenschappelijk onderwijs|vwo]]) at the Andreas College in [[Drachten]], he studied [[economics]] at the [[University of Groningen]]. In 1995 he was awarded a [[doctorate]] from the same institution, with his [[doctoral thesis]] concerning the differences in budget deficits and interest rates on government bonds between Members States of the [[European Union]].<ref>{{cite book | last = Knot | first = Klaas | title = Fiscal policy and interest rates in the European Community | publisher = Labyrint | location = Capelle a/d IJssel | year = 1995 | isbn = 9789072591272 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Knot | first1 = Klaas | last2 = de Haan | first2 = Jakob | title = Fiscal policy and interest rates in the European community | journal = [[European Journal of Political Economy]] | volume = 11 | issue = 1 |pages = 171–187 | doi = 10.1016/0176-2680(94)00012-9 | date = March 1995 }}</ref>

==Career==
After obtaining his doctorate Knot began working as a Senior Economist at De Nederlandsche Bank in the Department of Monetary and Economic Policy. In 1998 he left this post to become an economist in the European Department of the [[International Monetary Fund]] in [[Washington D.C.]]. A year later, he returned to De Nederlandsche Bank, as the Head of Banking and Supervisory Strategies Department. After serving in this capacity for three years, in December 2002 he left De Nederlandsche Bank again to become the Director of the Dutch Pensions and Insurance Authority, charged with supervision of all Dutch [[pension funds]] and [[insurance companies]]. In 2004 the Pensions and Insurance Authority was merged with De Nederlandsche Bank, and Knot was appointed as Director of the Supervisory Policy Division at De Nederlandsche Bank. In 2009 he left De Nederlandsche Bank once more to become Director of Financial Markets, and Deputy Treasurer General of the Dutch [[Ministry of Finance (Netherlands)|Ministry of Finance]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://nl.linkedin.com/pub/klaas-knot/11/839/83b |title=Klaas Knot |website=[[LinkedIn]] |archive-url=https://archive.md/fLpxV |archive-date=2021-11-08 |url-status=live}}</ref>{{Primary source inline|date=November 2021}} Knot has been the Vice Chairman of the [[Financial Stability Board]] since 2018 and is expected to replace Chairman [[Randal Quarles]] in 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-11-26|title=Appointment of new FSB Chair and Vice Chair|url=https://www.fsb.org/2018/11/appointment-of-new-fsb-chair-and-vice-chair/ |access-date=2021-11-08 |website=www.fsb.org|language=en-US}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Knot is married and has two children. He currently lives in [[Amsterdam]], which is where De Nederlandsche Bank is headquartered.

In 1999 and 2002 Knot ran the [[New York City Marathon|New York Marathon]] and the [[Amsterdam Marathon]], achieving times of  3.32.31 and 3.19.34 respectively.{{cn|date=June 2019}}
