Linda Papadopoulos (born May 10, 1971) is a Canadian psychologist based in England.


== Career ==
Papadopoulos was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, on May 10, 1971. She earned a BA degree in Psychology from York University in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, an MSc in Health psychology from the University of Surrey in the United Kingdom and a PhD in Psychology from City University in London.In 2010, she was commissioned by the Home Office to write a review on the effects of sexualisation on young people.Papadopoulos has hosted the TV series My Naked Secret on the Discovery Fit & Health channel. The series told the personal stories of people who are hiding physical abnormalities. She is also a regular commentator on many true life crime and history programmes on UKTV channels
