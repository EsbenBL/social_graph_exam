Victor Alexandre Ginsburgh (born 1939 in Rwanda-Urundi) is a Belgian economist.


== Biography ==
Ginsburgh was born in 1939 into an expatriate family: "My father was only a white Russian, and my mother an Austrian Jew".Victor Ginsburgh studied at the Free University of Brussels (now split into the Université Libre de Bruxelles and the Vrije Universiteit Brussel) and mastered in econometrics. He earned an economics PhD in 1972. He has been an Economics professor at Université Libre de Bruxelles since 1975.
Ginsburgh is the author or coauthor of papers on topics in applied and theoretical economics, including industrial organization and general equilibrium analysis.


== Political views ==
He is known in Belgium for his criticism towards Israel politics which he has expressed in numerous articles.
